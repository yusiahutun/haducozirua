How do I write a hurtful message to my boyfriend?

To avoid breeding disastrous misunderstandings in your relationship when communicating your dissatisfaction via texts, text when both of you are calm. Moreover, keep your messages short and precise, and be mindful of the emotional undertones of your words.

How do I say hurtful words to my boyfriend?

When sharing your frustrations with your boyfriend over text messages, you can start your messages with these phrases to create a platform for respectful communication between the two of you:

  • "I appreciate your effort." - e.g. I appreciate that you work so hard to support our family.
  • "Help me better understand this." - e.g., Please help me better understand your viewpoint.
  • "I'm listening..." - e.g., I want to hear your opinion, even if we disagree.
  • "I'm sorry." - e.g., I'm sorry for not communicating my feelings respectfully.
  • "I forgive you. Can you forgive me?" - e.g. I made a mistake and hope you can forgive me.
