Granger Smith Announces Yee Yee Nation Tour 2014

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  299 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Granger Smith is coming to your town, and he's bringing a party with him. The singer-songwriter has just announced his 2014 Yee Yee Nation Tour, which kicked off this week in Fargo, ND.

Smith is a newcomer in the mainstream sense, but with the best-selling independent country album in digital sales for 2013, the Texas boy is a well-kept secret that just got leaked. Smith is now taking his popular act on the road, hitting almost 20 cities across the U.S. in just one month. Along with his electric headlining performance, Logan Mize and Jeff Allen will join as openers throughout the Yee Yee Tour.

Oh yeah, and Earl Dibbles, Jr. will be there, too.

For more information on the Yee Yee Nation 2014 Tour or to buy tickets, visit Smith's official website.
