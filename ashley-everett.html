Biography

Ashley Everett was born in Chico, CA on April 19, 1989. Most well known as the singer Beyonce’s dance captain, Everett was one of the featured performers in the hit “Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)” music video. She participated in Usher‘s 2014 to 2015 UR Experience Tour. After performing with New York City’s renowned Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater, she turned down an offer to train at the Juilliard School in order to join the 2007 Beyonce Experience Tour.
