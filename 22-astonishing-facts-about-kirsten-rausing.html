FAQs

1. Who is Kirsten Rausing?

Kirsten Rausing is a Swedish-British billionaire businesswoman and philanthropist. She is the owner of Lanwades Stud, a prominent horse breeding operation, and is regarded as one of the leading figures in the equestrian industry.

Kirsten Rausing inherited her wealth from her grandfather, Ruben Rausing, who founded the Tetra Pak packaging company. Tetra Pak revolutionized the food and beverage packaging industry, thereby establishing the foundation for the family’s vast fortune.

Lanwades Stud is a renowned horse breeding operation located in Newmarket, England. It has gained international recognition for producing many successful racehorses and has become a prestigious name in the equestrian world.
