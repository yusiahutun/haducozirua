Delta Goodrem has uncharacteristically bared all in a racy shoot for Australia's largest men's magazine.

The 35-year-old swapped her girl-next-door appearance for something a little more risqué on Wednesday for the cover of Maxim's annual Hot 100 issue. 

The racy publication is notorious for featuring beautiful models, actresses and singers alike.

Her sun-kissed figure slightly tilted to the left, elongating her neck and making her collarbones pop.
