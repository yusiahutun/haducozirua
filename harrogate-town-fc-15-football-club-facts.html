Harrogate Town FC was founded in 1914.

Harrogate Town Football Club, also known as The Sulphurites, was established in 1914 and has a rich history spanning over a century. The club has remained a staple of the Harrogate community, providing excitement and entertainment for football enthusiasts.

The club’s home stadium is Wetherby Road.

Wetherby Road is the iconic home ground of Harrogate Town FC. Originally built in 1919, the stadium has undergone several renovations and improvements over the years to accommodate the growing fanbase and meet modern standards.

Harrogate Town FC currently competes in the English Football League (EFL) League Two.

After a series of successful seasons, Harrogate Town FC earned promotion to the English Football League in the 2020-2021 season. The club now competes in the highly competitive and prestigious EFL League Two.
