His extensive background in the culinary industry motivated him to get into the business. His first restaurant was named Alma Nove. Wahlburgers

In 2011, he teamed up with his brothers Mark and Donnie and opened the first Wahlburgers location. It was in Hingham, Massachusetts. It served burgers and hot dogs. Initially, the brothers thought the franchise would attract people who wanted to have a good time and enjoy their food. Little did they know the business would blow up.

Since its launch, it has been a decade, and the business has expanded tremendously. Currently, it has 49 outlets across the USA, Germany and Canada. Despite its success, Paul insists that the journey has been a rollercoaster.
