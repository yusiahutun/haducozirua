Kristanna Loken - Bio, Age, Career, Height, Net Worth, Facts

Talking about her professional career, Kristanna Loken began her modeling career at the age of 15, when she was discovered by a modeling scout. She began modeling in New York City, and quickly gained attention for her striking looks and tall stature. Moreover, she appeared in numerous fashion magazines and advertisements, including Calvin Klein, DKNY, and Armani.

Her modeling career helped launch her acting career, as she was discovered by a casting director while on a modeling job. She then landed her first film role in the 1997 movie “As Good as It Gets.” Although her acting career eventually took off, she continued to model throughout the early 2000s, appearing in advertisements for brands like L’Oreal and Versace.

She had a recurring role in the martial arts television series “Mortal Kombat: Conquest” (1999-2000). Her breakthrough role came in the 2003 film “Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines” she played the villainous T-X, a Terminator sent back in time to kill John Connor. For the role, the actress underwent a combat course in the Israeli army. Moreover, the costume of Т-Х in Terminator 3 was auctioned in London in the year 2017.

Other roles

In the 2005 movie “BloodRayne”, she starred as the titular character, a half-human, half-vampire warrior seeking revenge against her vampire father. Later, she played Elora in the 2007 film “In the Name of the King: A Dungeon Siege Tale.” Loken had a recurring role in the fifth season of “The L Word” (2007-2008) and the series is about a group of lesbian and bisexual women in Los Angeles. In 2007, she appeared as Jane Vasco in “Painkiller Jane” a television series based on a comic book of the same name.

She had a guest role in the 2009 spy thriller series “Burn Notice” as CIA agent Rebecca Lang, and would reprise that role in three sixth-season episodes during mid-2012. Later, she starred in 2014 in the movie “Mercenaries”, alongside Cynthia Rothrock, Brigitte Nielsen, Vivica A. Fox, Zoë Bell, and Nicole Bilderback. She co-starred in the 2017 romantic thriller film “Body of Deceit,” alongside Sarai Givaty.

Loken has also lent her voice to several video games and animated TV shows, including “Star Wars: The Clone Wars,” “Lego: Jurassic World,” and “Terminator 3: Redemption.”

Kristanna Loken – Awards

In her acting career, she has won a couple of awards and nominations. In 2004, she won the Short Film Award for Best Supporting Actress. Moreover, she won the 2008 Feature Film Award for Best Supporting Actress. Meanwhile, in 2004, she was nominated for an MTV Movie Award and two Saturn Awards.

Net Worth 2023

Throughout her acting and modeling career, she has collected a good sum of money. As far as now, her net worth is estimated at around $8 million while her annual salary, income, and salary are still missing.

Relationship Status

Talking about her relationship status, Kristanna Loken is currently divorced and single. In the past, she was married to her Painkiller Jane co-star Noah Danby on the 10th of May 2008 at her family’s farm.

However, she announced she had separated from Danby and was dating a woman in an interview published on November 16, 2009. In May 2016, Loken gave birth to a son whose name is still unknown. Moreover, she also dated Justin Whalin (1995 – 1998) and Michelle Rodriguez (2006 – 2007).
