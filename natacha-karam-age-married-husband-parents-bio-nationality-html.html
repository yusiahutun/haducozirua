Bio Reveals Age, Details On Parents

The rising star, Natacha Karam, 23 of age, happens to be of British nationality; working and living in Los Angeles. The actress is of both French Lebanese and Northern Irish heritage. 

As a child, she traveled a lot with her parents and her brother Christophe Karam, due to her dad's job. Before she turned 13, Natacha lived in five different countries. She finally moved to London when she was 18 and made it her self-claimed home even if she is not from around there.
