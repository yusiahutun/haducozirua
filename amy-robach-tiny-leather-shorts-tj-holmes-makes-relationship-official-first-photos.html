Amy Robach rocks tiny leather shorts as T.J. Holmes makes relationship official with first loved-up

 Posted on February 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  563 words  |  Aldo Pusey

It's official! Amy Robach and T.J. Holmes have made their hard launch as a couple on social media after subtly hinting at their new romance over the past few months.

In new photos shared on Instagram, T.J. finally revealed that he and Amy were together with affectionate snaps from their appearance Donald W. Reynolds Razorback Stadium in Arkansas over the weekend.

The couple were in 46-year-old T.J.'s home state catching a football game at the University of Arkansas, and they couldn't have looked cozier.

They held onto each other's hands as Amy rocked an incredible ensemble, showcasing her ultra-toned legs in a pair of tiny leather shorts and a bronze satin camisole, while T.J. rocked a dapper blazer and slacks.

In one of the snaps, T.J. was even seeing closely holding onto his girlfriend, 50, while grabbing her waist from behind, almost reminiscent of the paparazzi shots that first revealed their relationship back in November.

Amy and T.J. were absent from any form of social media for nearly ten months, with Amy's last post coming in November of 2022 from her family vacation to Prague over the Thanksgiving break, just days before news of her romance broke.

The former GMA star, best known for her previous co-hosting gig with T.J. on the ABC News show GMA3: What You Need to Know, finally returned to Instagram on August 28 with a cryptic post.

The photo simply featured two pairs of feet in running gear and sneakers, with a caption that implied Amy was training for the NYC Marathon. While it was unclear at first, when T.J. reposted the same photo, it essentially announced that they were in it together.

The romance first made headlines back in November, when pictures of the amorous pair revealed they were apparently carrying out an affair. At the time, both were married, Amy to actor Andrew Shue and T.J. to attorney Marilee Fiebig.

While Amy and Andrew were seemingly separated earlier, they remained quiet about their estrangement. Marilee and T.J., meanwhile, filed for divorce not long after and just recently settled the terms of their divorce.

The former couple share ten-year-old daughter Sabine, while Amy shares 21-year-old Ava Monroe and 16-year-old Annalise with ex-husband Tim McIntosh.

During her marriage to Andrew, best known for starring in Melrose Place in the '90s, she also acted as step-mom to his sons from his previous marriage, Nate, Wyatt, and Aidan.

Marilee has sparingly shared posts that allude to her new life away from her ex, although they've both continued to be as supportive as ever of their daughter.
