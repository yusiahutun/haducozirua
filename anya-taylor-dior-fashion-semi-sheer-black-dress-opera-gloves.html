Anya Taylor-Joy Attends Dior's Pre-Fall Show in a Dramatic LBD and Opera Gloves

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  427 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The birthday festivities came early this year for Anya Taylor-Joy, who turns 28 years old today, April 16! On the eve of her special day, she spent a night out with Dior.

Taylor-Joy has served as a global brand ambassador for the French fashion house for almost three years now, so it was no surprise to see her sitting front row at the brand’s Pre-Fall 2024 show Monday night at the Brooklyn Museum. And although she was wearing all black for the occasion, she looked absolutely radiant.

Nina Westervelt//Getty Images

Nina Westervelt//Getty Images

The Golden Globe winner brought the drama in an inky-black sleeveless midi dress that featured a squared-off strapless neckline with a second scoop neckline in a sheer fabric attached to it, frilly details along the top hem, a glossy narrow leather belt, and a pencil skirt. For some extra grandeur, she added matching sheer black opera gloves, tall pointed-toe stiletto heels, and a wide black headband that she wore over her long, blonde ponytail. Taylor-Joy even went funeral chic for her makeup, which included a mesmerizing dark smoky eye, black eyeliner, a tangerine-orange tinted lip, soft pink blush, and a deep-brown manicure. She finished everything off with minimal jewelry, wearing her gorgeous gold wedding ring, set with diamonds and an emerald.

Taylor-Joy has been leaning into a dark palette recently—entering her rocker era, dare we say? Her shift to a more grungy wardrobe is likely inspired by her forthcoming movie Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga, in which she plays Imperator Furiosa, a member of a biker horde and a younger incarnation of the character Charlize Theron introduced in 2015’s Mad Max: Fury Road. Earlier this week, Taylor-Joy stepped out for a New York Knicks game with husband Malcolm McRae in sexy, glossy leather pants that featured a cutout down the center of each leg with a lace-up pattern within. She paired the daring bottoms with a ribbed white tank top, layered under a fitted black leather motorcycle jacket, along with stiletto boots and cat-eye shades. Prior to that, the Queen’s Gambit star attended CinemaCon in Las Vegas in a daring black leather dress from Ludovic de Saint Sernin’s Fall/Winter 2024 ready-to-wear collection. The piece looked like one long studded belt that wrapped around her body, creating a crisscross halter neckline and a decorative strip that hung off the bottom hem.
