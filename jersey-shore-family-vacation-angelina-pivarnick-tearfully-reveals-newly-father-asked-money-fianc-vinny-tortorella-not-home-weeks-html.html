Angelina Pivarnick tearfully revealed that her newly found biological father asked her for money on Thursday's episode of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation on MTV.

The 37-year-old reality while visiting Mike 'The Situation' Sorrentino, 41, and his wife Lauren Pesce also shared that her fiancé Vinny Tortorella had raised multiple red flags.

Angelina with Mike's help met her biological father, but cried as she told them how trouble started after their first meeting when he stopped to admire her Rolex.

'I wish I could afford a watch like that,' her father was shown saying in a flashback.

'I really didn't want to think anything of it,' Angelina told Mike and Lauren. 'But then his dog had this big infection. And he was saying he didn't have money for the dog. So I took him to the vet that I go to, and it was $1,000,' Angelina said.
