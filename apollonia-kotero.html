Apollonia Kotero’s Childhood and Education

Apollonia Kotero was born Patricia Kotero on August 2, 1959, in Santa Monica, California, to parents Victor Kotero and Socorro Koterio, the former a restaurant manager and the latter a caregiver for the elderly. Her parents were Mexican immigrants.

Kotero is of Hispanic ethnicity and is an American. She dropped out of high school at the age of 16 to pursue modeling and acting.

Apollonia Kotero’s Professional Career

Apollonia Kotero launched her modeling career after winning the title of “Miss San Pedro.” She worked as a cheerleader for the pro-American football team Los Angeles Rams in the early 1980s. She made her acting debut in films such as CHiPs, Tales of the Gold Monkey, Matt Houston, Fantasy Island, and Knight Rider.

Kotero appeared in the music videos of hit singer/songwriters Ray Parker Jr. and Eddie Money from the 1990s. She made her television debut in 1984 with the ABC TV movie The Mystic Warrior, in which she played the Native American woman “Wicahpi.”

Her big break came when late singer Prince signed her to his girl group, Vanity 6. She was the lead actress in Purple Rain (1984). For the film, the group was renamed Apollonia 6. Personal Life of Apollonia Kotero

Apollonia Kotero may be single right now, but she has been married twice before. Kazja, a businessman, was her first husband, whom she married on February 16, 1980. However, the couple was unable to manage their relationship and divorced in 1985, citing irreconcilable differences.

She met Kevin Bernhardt, an actor, screenwriter, and producer while working on the same project after her divorce. They were good friends at first, but after a few meetings, they fell in love and married. In an intimate ceremony, the couple exchanged vows in 1987.

This, like her previous relationship, did not last long, and the couple divorced in 1997 after ten years of marriage on mutual terms.

She was once linked to the late singer Prince. Their romance rumors spread like wildfire after the duo collaborated on the 1984 film Purple Rain. Despite this, the couple never confirmed their rumored relationship.
