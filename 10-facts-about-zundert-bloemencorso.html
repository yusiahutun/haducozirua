Zundert Bloemencorso is the largest flower parade in the world.

Every year, the small town of Zundert in the Netherlands hosts the grand spectacle of Zundert Bloemencorso, attracting thousands of visitors from all over the globe.

The bloemencorso dates back to 1936.

The Zundert Bloemencorso tradition started in 1936 as a way to celebrate the region’s rich horticultural heritage. Since then, it has become an annual tradition that brings the community together.

Over 20 gigantic floats participate in the parade each year.

The highlight of Zundert Bloemencorso is the impressive floats, which are created by various neighborhoods in Zundert. These floats are made entirely of flowers and can reach heights of up to 20 meters.

More than three million flowers are used in the parade.

Zundert Bloemencorso is a floral extravaganza, with the floats decorated using millions of dahlias. The vibrant colors and intricate designs make the parade a visual feast for spectators.

The theme of the parade changes every year.

Each year, the Zundert Bloemencorso committee selects a unique theme, which serves as the inspiration for the floats. This allows for creativity and innovation in the design and presentation of the parade.

The preparation for the parade begins months in advance.

The creation of the Zundert Bloemencorso floats is a labor-intensive process that requires meticulous planning and coordination. The participating neighborhoods start working on their designs and floral arrangements several months before the actual parade.

Award-winning designers and builders participate in the parade.

Zundert Bloemencorso attracts talented designers and builders who strive to create the most stunning floats. The dedication and skill of these individuals contribute to the grandeur of the event.

There are various activities and events during Bloemencorso weekend.

Aside from the parade, Zundert Bloemencorso offers a range of activities and events for visitors to enjoy. These include live music performances, art exhibitions, local food stalls, and a carnival atmosphere throughout the town.

The parade attracts visitors from all over the world.

Zundert Bloemencorso has gained international recognition, drawing visitors from different countries to experience the beauty and splendor of the flower parade. This event has become a must-see attraction for flower and festival enthusiasts.

Zundert Bloemencorso promotes unity and community spirit.

The creation and participation in Zundert Bloemencorso foster a sense of unity and pride within the community. It brings people together to celebrate their shared passion for flowers and showcase their talents to the world.
