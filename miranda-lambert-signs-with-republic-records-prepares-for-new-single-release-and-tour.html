Miranda Lambert Signs With Republic Records, Prepares For New Single Release and Tour

 Posted on January 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  522 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – Miranda Lambert, the trailblazing superstar and songwriter known for her candid approach to country music, has entered a new creative partnership to elevate her career further. Concluding her successful 48-show Velvet Rodeo residency in Las Vegas, Lambert, the most awarded artist in Academy of Country Music (ACM) history, including their top Entertainer of the Year honor, has inked a deal with Republic Records.

The collaboration with Nashville-based Big Loud encompasses various promotional and marketing efforts, including Country Radio promotion. This strategic partnership comes hot on the heels of Lambert’s highly acclaimed 2022 album Palomino and marks her first solo venture since then.

Her latest track, “Wranglers,” which will be released on May 3, is a testament to Lambert’s resilience and empowerment. As she describes it, “‘Wranglers’ is a tale of a woman taking her power back. I think we can all identify with the character in this song because we have all had a time in our lives when we needed to find a way to find our strength and also get a little revenge on someone who did us wrong or hurt us.”

Lambert’s excitement for this new chapter in her musical journey is palpable. She expresses, “Having a new home has given me a hunger I didn’t realize I still had inside me. This song could be on the same record as ‘Gunpowder & Lead’; it has that same fury.”

Republic Records Founder and Chairman Monte Lipman shares Lambert’s enthusiasm, stating, “Miranda Lambert’s legacy as both a consummate storyteller and legendary performer speaks for itself. Her new music is spectacular, and we are thrilled and honored to welcome her to Republic Records in partnership with Big Loud.”

In addition to her musical endeavors, Lambert is gearing up for live performances, including an eagerly awaited appearance at the Stagecoach festival on April 27. Fans worldwide can tune into the Amazon Music livestream of her set and listen to her full performance on SiriusXM’s The Highway.

Lambert’s tour dates span the summer, with stops at iconic venues and festivals nationwide. Fans can catch her electrifying performances from April through September from Texas to Montana.
