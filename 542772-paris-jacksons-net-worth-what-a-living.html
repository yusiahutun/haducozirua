What is Paris Jackson’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth and CM Xray, Jackson's net worth is around $150 million. She has amassed this wealth through various sources, including her inheritance from her father, the late Michael Jackson, who left a significant estate to his children.

Paris and her siblings receive an $8 million annual allowance and will inherit equal shares of their father's estate when they reach certain ages.

The personality has also built her fortune through her successful career as a model, actress, and musician. Paris signed with IMG Models in 2017 and has graced the covers of prestigious magazines like Rolling Stone, Harper’s Bazaar Spain, and Vogue.

She has also ventured into music, forming a folk band called The Soundflowers and releasing her debut album, Wilted, under Republic Records. Paris has also pursued acting, appearing in TV series like Star. She has starred in projects like American Horror Story, The Space Between, and Habit.

How much money did Paris Jackson inherit from Michael Jackson?

Paris Jackson inherited a substantial amount of money from Michael Jackson. According to Distractify, her father split his estate equally among his three children: Paris, Prince, and Prince Michael II (Blanket).

At the time of Michael Jackson's death in 2009, his assets were worth $111 million, although the IRS initially estimated his estate at $482 million. Paris and her siblings receive an $8 million allowance annually.

Paris and her siblings will inherit half of their late father's estate when they turn 33, with the rest coming when they turn 40. Michael Jackson left a 40% interest in his estate to his mother, 40% to his three children, and the remaining 20% to various children's charities.
