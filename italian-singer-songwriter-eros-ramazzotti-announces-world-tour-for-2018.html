Italian Singer-Songwriter Eros Ramazzotti Announces World Tour For 2018

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  526 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Italian singer-songwriter Eros Ramazzotti announced a new round of dates for his Vita Ce N’È World Tour, including a string of North American shows.

The tour, which has already shifted more than 200,000 tickets for the initial dates of his European segment, will cross five continents before all is said and done.

The first segment of the tour kicks off on February 17th with the first of a pair of sold-out show at the Olympiahalle in Munich and will hit major markets in Europe, including Rome, Madrid, London, and Amsterdam before concluding on April 15th at the Stadthalle in Vienna.

Then in May, Ramazzotti heads across the pond for a tour of the Western Hemisphere, starting at the Auditoria Telmex in Guadalajara on May 15th, and wrapping on June 26th at the Rosemont Theatre in Chicago.

In September, he’ll return to Europe for a tour of the Balkans, Eastern Europe, and Scandinavia before concluding with a performance at Ericsson Globe Stockholm on Oct. 30th.

The North American tour segment will be produced by Live Nation, while CTS Eventim’s Vertigo is lined up to handle the European tour segments.

Ramazzotti is touring behind his latest album Vita ce n’è. Released on Nov. 23rd via Polydor, the album features collaborations with Alessia Cara and Luis Fonsi and is already #1 on FIMI’s Italian albums chart.
