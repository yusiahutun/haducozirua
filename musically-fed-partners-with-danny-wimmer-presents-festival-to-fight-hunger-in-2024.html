Musically Fed Partners With Danny Wimmer Presents Festivals To Fight Hunger In 2024

 Posted on March 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  375 words  |  Aldo Pusey

PHOENIX (CelebrityAccess) – Musically Fed, a non-profit organization based in Phoenix, committed to tackling hunger through partnerships within the music industry, has revealed its participation in the forthcoming Danny Wimmer Presents (DWP) festivals slated for 2024. The organization will be actively present at these events to salvage unused catering meant for artists and staff, channeling it towards providing essential meals to local non-profits in the respective host cities of each festival.

Chamie McCurry, Chief Marketing Officer of DWP, articulated, “Constructing a festival is akin to nurturing a city from scratch, one that thrives on music and solidarity. Our pride doesn’t merely stem from the stages erected or the performances staged. It’s in the enduring impact we leave on the communities we touch. Collaborating with Musically Fed, we give back, aiding the fight against food insecurity and contributing positively to the cities that graciously welcome us annually. It’s beyond music; it’s about fostering community, sustainability, and effecting change where it truly matters.”

Since its inception in 2016, Musically Fed has galvanized the music industry to combat hunger by liaising with artists, promoters, management, and venues to donate surplus backstage meals, including VIP and artist catering, to community organizations nationwide. Through its endeavors, the organization has furnished over 550,000 meals to needy individuals throughout America.

“We are thrilled to extend our collaboration with Danny Wimmer Presents, a distinguished festival producer in the nation, for the third consecutive year,” expressed Musically Fed’s founder, Maria Brunner. “It’s a privilege to collaborate with a team devoted to addressing sustainability and food insecurity in the communities they visit, all while delivering a captivating and diverse lineup of artists.”

Musically Fed is slated to be present at the following DWP festivals throughout the year, endeavoring to engender a lasting positive impact in each host city:

May 9-12, 2024: Welcome to Rockville at Daytona International Speedway, Daytona, FL
May 16-19, 2024: Sonic Temple at Historic Crew Stadium, Columbus, OH
July 19-21, 2024: Inkcarceration at Ohio State Reformatory, Mansfield, OH
Sept 19-22, 2024: Bourbon & Beyond at Highland Festival Grounds, Louisville, KY
Sept 25-29, 2024: Louder Than Life at Highland Festival Grounds, Louisville, KY
Oct 10-13, 2024: Aftershock at Discovery Park, Sacramento, CA
Oct 18-20, 2024: Golden Sky at Discovery Park, Sacramento, CA
