Buy a Ryobi Battery Kit and Get a Free Power Tool With This Deal

If you have DIY jobs around that house that need to get done, now is the perfect time to buy some new power tools, replace an old drill, or stock up on power tool battery packs. Labor Day weekend is here, and that means deals are readily available. If you're a fan of Ryobi, Home Depot's free tool deal is back.

This is honestly one of the best power tool deals, and I took advantage of it earlier this summer. Home Depot will give you a free tool for Labor Day weekend when you buy a Ryobi battery and charger combo kit. You have until EOD on September 4th to order. You're already getting a solid deal by picking up three battery packs, a charger, and a carry case, all for $199, which is often around $300. Then, you get to add another tool to your collection.

Better yet, it's not some terrible tool you'll never use. Instead, the website will let you choose from a wide assortment, including a Ryobi Nail Gun, brushless 1/2-inch Hammer Drill, Impact Drivers, Angle grinders, Jig-Saws, or even a Shop Vac.

Ryobi / Home Depot

You can spend $199 and get something like an Extended Reach Cordless Ratchet, three battery packs, a charger, and a carry case. That's one heck of a package. And if you're new to power tools, we recommend a good cordless drill or impact driver. Then, you'll have a solid base to start growing your collection to fill a toolbox.

For those unaware, you should always stick to one brand of cordless power tools because one Ryobi battery works with nearly 300 different devices. Once you're in the Ryobi ecosystem, you can continuously buy new tools for every job. Another reason this is a solid deal is that you're getting three different-size batteries from the official source. This is important because you shouldn't buy off-brand tool battery packs. They're not the best idea and could even ruin your tool, which is why they're always cheaper than the real thing.

If you want to finish some jobs around the house or expand your ever-growing collection of battery-powered tools, this deal ends on September 4th. For those who only need a battery, grab this 2-pack for only $79.00.
