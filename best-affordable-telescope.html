Types of Affordable Telescopes

This list is comprised of two types of telescopes: reflector telescopes and refractor telescopes. These are more affordable than other types of telescopes, but still offer endless features.

  • A reflecting telescope uses a single or a combination of curved mirrors that reflect light and form an image that you see from the scope. This type of telescope is a favorite to most astronomers due to its simplistic mechanisms and accurate results.
  • A refractor telescope uses two lenses to focus on light. These lenses are ‘convex’ shaped, which makes the object you’re focusing on appear closer to you than it is in reality. These are most suitable for viewing planets.
