Who is Dolly from My 600-Lb?

Dolly is a Fort Worth native and one of the cast members on My 600-Lb Life show on TLC. For the better of her life growing up, she had been struggling with several challenges, including:

  • Breathing problems
  • Congestive heart failure

Like many other participants on the television show, her unhealthy relationship with food started when she was still a little girl. Her father left her mother after becoming a drug addict leaving her mother to raise them single-handedly.

The situation became worse while she was in kindergarten, where she was diagnosed with several health conditions, such as:

With all the health and family challenges she faced, she felt abandoned, thus turning to food for solace. At seven years, Dolly weighed 120 lbs. Unfortunately, her obesity resulted in constant bullying and a vicious food consumption cycle which saw her cross the 300-lb mark at 16 years.
