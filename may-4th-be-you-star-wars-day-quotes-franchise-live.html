May the 4th be with you on Star Wars Day: Quotes from the franchise to live by - YP

 Posted on October 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  399 words  |  Martina Birk

Happy Star Wars Day! Popularised by fans of the sci-fi franchise, the day originates from the pun “May the 4th be with you,” a variant of the popular Star Wars catchphrase “May the force be with you”.

As today is a day to celebrate everything from the universe created in 1977 by filmmaker George Lucas, let’s take a look at a few quotes from the films and absorb some wisdom from Yoda, Han Solo and more.

1. “Do or do not. There is no try.” – Yoda

This is arguably one of the most popular lines from the films, straight from the Jedi master himself. You can use it when someone needs a little tough love.

2. “Your focus determines your reality.” – Qui-Gon Jinn

The words of another Jedi master remind you to take control of your fate.

3. “In my experience, there is no such thing as luck.” – Obi-Wan Kenobi

One of the most powerful Jedis reminds you that hard work pays off, but sitting around waiting for chance does not.

The most sacred of holidays. Photo: Shutterstock

4. “Your eyes can deceive you. Don’t trust them.” – Obi-Wan Kenobi

Another saying from Obi-Wan that tells you to go with your gut feeling.

5. “I find your lack of faith disturbing.” – Darth Vader

Even though he is the lead villain in the franchise, he still taught us not to doubt ourselves.

6. “The greatest teacher, failure is.” – Yoda

It’s a simple lesson – we learn from our mistakes.

7. “Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering.” – Yoda

Yoda showed us the power of fear, how it affects us and how it can impact the people around us.

8. “Never tell me the odds!” – Han Solo

Use this whenever someone tells you that something cannot be done.

Who knew one franchise could teach you so much? Photo: Shutterstock

9. “Someone has to save our skins.” – Princess Leia

The heroine took the reins of her own rescue and reminded us that sometimes, you need to take control of your own life.

10. “May the force be with you.” – General Jan Dodonna

Last but not least, you can use this famous quote to wish someone good luck.
