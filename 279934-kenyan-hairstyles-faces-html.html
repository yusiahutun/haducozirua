1. Rope twists

Rope twist is one of the round faces hairstyles that best suits your type of face. You can either choose to release the hair or to hold it back and release a few on the front line. This hairstyle brings out the stunning look that will leave everyone admiring you. The style is appropriate for the family function or even for the church service. With the bulky look from the Havana twists, round faced women will automatically look stunning. Wearing a bulky-looking hairstyle will make a round face appear relatively longer and eye-catching. Crochet Braids form part of the current Kenyan hairstyles that most women admire not only for their simplicity but also for being a protective hairstyle.
