CNN presenter Richard Quest gets engaged to gay lover

It is crystal clear popular CNN business journalist Richard Quest means business, quiet literally.

This is after the well-manicured journalist went down on one knee and popped the big question to his gay lover.

In an Instagram post seen by TUKO.co.ke on Quest's Instagram on Monday, May 27, he revealed how pleased he was after his lover accepted his marriage proposal.

''I asked, and he said “yes”'' he captioned a photo of himself with his lover, both grinning to the cameras.

Following his big announcement, his online fans from different parts of the world took to the comments section of his post and showered the pair with endless congratulatory messages.

The Quest Means Business presenter went down on one knee to ask for his lover's hand in marriage barely months after he advocated for gay rights during his tour in Kenya.

As reported by TUKO.co.ke earlier, Quest who was in Nairobi to take part in the inaugural KQ flight from Kenya to US also caused a stir after he attended a church service at the Bishop Allan Kiuna led Jubilee Christian Church where he even addressed the congregation.
