Sanctuary Grove Collectible - God Of War Ragnarok Wiki Guide

 Posted on September 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  510 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

God of War Ragnarok Sanctuary Grove region is Freya’s home in the Midgard Realm, where Odin cursed her to live for eternity.

So, you will visit Sanctuary Grove for the first time playing as Atreus during The Runaway Main Quest when you try to seek shelter from the cold weather. During your short visit there, you won’t be able to find any collectibles.

But, once you finish the main story, the region becomes again available to explore and features only a collectible which is part of the Nine Realms In Bloom Favour covered here.

Without further ado, let’s see how to unlock the Sanctuary Grove region and where to find the Nine Realms in Bloom collectible in the video game developed by Santa Monica Studios.

How To Unlock Sanctuary Grove

The most important requirement to unlock the Sanctuary Grove region is to finish the main story.

Then, fast travel to Sindri’s House and hit the bell hanging on the tree using your Leviathan Axe to call Ratatoskr the Squirrel.

After you finish talking with Ratatoskr, pick the pouch of Yggdrasil seeds in front of you. If you don’t see it there, you might’ve already got it.

Now, go to the Mystic Gateway at Sindri’s House, select the Midgard Realm and unlock the Undiscovered Mystic Gateway at Sanctuary Grove by holding (X).

Nine Realms In Bloom (Mirkweed Flower): Beside Chaurli

After you reach Sanctuary Grove, follow Freya to assist at the reunion between her and Chaurli, the Giant Turtle, which holds Freya’s Home beneath him.

To the left of Chaurli, you’ll find the Mirkweed Flower as part of the Nine Realms In Bloom Favour we mentioned earlier.

That sums up everything about how you can unlock the Sanctuary Grove and where to find the Nine Realms In Bloom collectible in God of War Ragnarok.
