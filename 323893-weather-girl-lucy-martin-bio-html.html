Lucy Martin - the weather presenter's biography

How old is Lucy Martin weather presenter? The journalist is 31 years old (as of 2022), just months shy from her 32nd birthday. She was born on 5th May 1990 in Whiston, Merseyside, United Kingdom. The BBC weather presenter was born with only one hand and says that did not deter her from experiencing the joys and activities of childhood.

Education

After completing her high school studies, the weather presenter joined Durham University and graduated with a bachelor's degree in geography. Her course choice explains her passion for weather journalism. She says that she always had an interest in weather-related issues and everything that came with it.

As part of her geography degree, she studied meteorology and says that it is one of those subjects that make one want to learn more.

Did Lucy ever have a prosthetic arm?

Yes, the weather presenter had a prosthetic arm for a short while when she was in elementary school. Her mother took her to the hospital and had her fitted with a bionic arm. However, Lucy did not adapt well to using the arm. She spent most of her time taking it off and placing it in her toy box.

Later, her mother had her fitted with a prosthetic arm. Lucy would go to appointments to learn how to use the arm. Like the bionic arm, she did not get on with the prosthetic one. She took it to school one day and had a show and tell before deciding she did not want it.

That marked the end of bionic and prosthetic arms for the weather presenter. She never got another one since then.

How did Lucy join BBC?

She was among the applicants for BBC's initiative that targeted physically challenged people. The initiative saw thousands of applicants looking to join the British corporation. Lucy was among the few applicants chosen to undergo journalism training and join the BBC. The available positions were in the environment and weather departments.

Is Lucy Martin married?

Yes, BBC's Lucy Martin is a married woman. Lucy Martin's husband is Stewart Castledine. The two got married in 2000 and have a son named Charlie. Their son was born on 8th August 2020. A few weeks after, the presenter posted a photo of her son on Instagram to her fans' adoration.

Lucy Martin's Icon Award

In 2017, Lucy was nominated for the Icon Awards. The award recognizes people who champion diversity in various sectors. Lucy was nominated for her representation of disabled persons in the television and media industry.
