Khloe Kardashian admits she enjoyed it when 'beautiful' sister Kim got dumped

 Posted on May 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  565 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It is the sort of development that usually makes siblings come together.

But Khloe Kardashian has revealed she used to enjoy it when her 'beautiful' sister Kim got dumped.

She made the shock admission as she tried to comfort a man on her new show who had just been dumped by his boyfriend.

'I used to be like, "OK, I’m sorry you’re not feeling well Kim."
