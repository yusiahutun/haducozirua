Betrayal has been among us since the world was created and for sure it is still there till now. Well, from all that, we can expect betrayal quotes, betrayal messages and even betrayal songs to exist and as sure as we still believe in oxygen, they do exist.

Taking the Bible as an example, for instance, the story of how Judas betrayed Jesus for thirty pieces of silver is well known. There is a phrase people use when they think you are about to betray them. The person would say something like, “ don’t be a Judas”. We get betrayed by family, friends and even our lovers. At other times, we are the people betraying other people. With that said and done, below are some betrayal quotes on family, friendships and love.
