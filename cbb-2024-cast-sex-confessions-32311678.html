Celebrity Big Brother is back - and it’s looking to be its most dramatic yet.

The ITV show kicked off this week - making its return to ITV screens after six years. Fronted by AJ Odudu and Will Best, the pair have welcomed a slew of famous faces into the most famous house in Britain.

The likes of Love Island star Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu, Corrie legend Colson Smith and X Factor judge Louis Walsh have all signed up for the new series. They have been joined by This Morning’s Fern Britton, Levi Roots and ZeZe Mills who will be living together for three whole weeks.

Meanwhile, reality TV icon Sharon Osbourne is the Celebrity Big Brother lodger. A first for the series, and as part of being a lodger, Sharon will only stay in the house for a brief amount of time and she also has her own bedroom.

But it turns out some of the Celebrity Big Brother 2024 cast are not shy when it comes to sharing the details of their bedroom antics. In fact, several famous faces have spoken out about their sex lives and what goes on under the sheets…

'Three bonks a day'

Sharon, married to rocker legend Ozzy, admitted the couple used to be at it multiple times a day and probably went too hard, so to speak. She got candid about their bedroom activity during a chat on The Talk a few years prior.

Host Sheryl Underwood asked: "How many times a day are you and Ozzy getting it? How many times is Ozzy walking up on you in the kitchen?” Sharon replied: "We all know we oversexed, it's not a secret. He had enough for all... he liked to share.

“It used to be three times a day, but it's much less now. Let's say a couple of times a week. Which is normal in a long, long relationship."

Ozzy's affairs have included liaisons with his hairstylist, two masseuses, a cook, and a Russian teen. Discussing his sex addiction, Sharon added: "There's nothing [Ozzy] is not addicted to. I mean, that's his personality".

'Busy sex life'

Levi Roots shocked his fellow housemates, and viewers, when he made a surprising revelation about his family life earlier this week.

Marisha Wallace asked the star how many children he has. Levi replied: “Eight” as his co-stars gasped in shock. Fern Britton exclaimed: “Man alive.” Levi admitted: “Fern it gets worse. I have eight children with seven different mothers.”

Marisha joked: “God almighty, you did a lot.” Levi then explained that his eldest child is in their 40s. He shared: “I am 65, I started young.” Marisha then quipped: “Levi you probably are my daddy," as the pair burst into laughter.

Romping a ghost

Lauren once confessed she had sex with a ghost but her deceased beau dumped her after two romps in the sack. In 2020, the Real Housewives of Cheshire star paid a visit to a psychic.

She explained: “I have to admit something really weird. I’ve had sex with a ghost.” As pal Nicole rolled her eyes, Lauren insisted: “I actually properly had sex. I knew it wasn’t a dirty dream because it was too real, like something was touching me.”

But this isn’t the first time the star got intimate with a ghostly presence. She went on: “I have had that before where I feel like somebody’s holding me down and I can’t get up.” Clearly confused by her bizarre confession, Nicole immediately said “That’s weird” before the physic disagreed adding: “That’s not weird, that happens”. Lauren continued: “This particular ghost that came and had sex.

It happened twice, it was a very bizarre feeling, but he never came back. Does that mean I wasn’t good in bed?” The psychic explained that she had been ghosted by the mystical figure and reassured her it was “normal”.

'Sex act' on TV

Ekin-Su shot to fame back in 2022 thanks to her appearance on Love Island. She ended up winning the show with her then-beau Davide Sanclimenti.

And it’s fair to say the pair had a rather rocky, and saucy, relationship in the villa. But during one night on the show, it appeared that Davide and Ekin got up to something a little X-rated under the duvet.

The following morning, actress Ekin was quick to fill her pals in on what went down the night before. Gemma Owen quizzed her: "Ekin, what happened in bed with you and Davide last night?"

Smiling, Ekin-Su said: "Absolutely nothing." The girls then jokingly called her a liar, to which Ekin cheekily added: "I blow-dried something. It dried in exactly 10 seconds."

'Surprising' sex life

Lauren’s pal and Real Housewives of Cheshire star Ester Dee has said Lauren has a rather “surprising" sex life. When asked if she would spill any more secrets, Ester replied: "One hundred per cent when it comes to the bedroom!”

She added to OK!: “She’s told me stuff and my mouth is open and I say “no! Him?”, and she’ll say “Yes!”. You’d be surprised who she’s slept with.

"Obviously I can’t say who, that’s up to Lauren by oh my! With Lauren, you always get lots of glorious details!"
