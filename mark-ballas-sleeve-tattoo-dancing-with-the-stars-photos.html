Mark Ballas Gets Giant Sleeve Tattoo!

 Posted on June 24, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  124 words  |  Larita Shotwell

When Mark Ballas hits the stage for "Dancing with the Stars" next week, he'll be doing it with some new ink on his fit bod!

The 26-year-old professional dancer celebrated surviving the chopping block with Bristol Palin by finishing off a massive arm sleeve tattoo.

"At @MisterCtoons shop bout to finish my Tatt up," he tweeted last night along with a pic of him under the tattoo needle. "Nice to be with the homies and forget the negatives."

He later posted another photo of the finished product ... and his impressive torso!

We don't know what exactly the tattoo is of, but we see a frog, guitar and some writing in there.
