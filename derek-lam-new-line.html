Derek Lam Handbags - Derek Lam 10 Crosby Line

Media Platforms Design Team

The timing couldn't be more fortuitous—in step with BAZAAR's Well-Spent Dollar issue, Derek Lam 10 Crosby, the downtown New York designer's contemporary diffusion line, is introducing a tightly edited collection of bags. The streetwise lineup stars a capacious tote, Donald Judd-inspired, color-blocked clutches, half-keg-shaped back-packs, and cute mini pouches. Launched for resort alongside the brand's relaxed-meets-business looks, these smart investments may just be your new go-to's. "Modern, chic functionality was the overall inspiration," says Lam. And he's clearly walking the walk, focusing on user-friendly details like flat sides that contour the body and blessedly ample storage space. It's a subject on which the designer gets rather lyrical: "Each bag, especially the Sullivan tote, reflects the new-world gadgets and items we use every day—iPad, iPhone, et cetera—but still has enough room to hold decidedly 'old-school' items like a favorite paperback," he explains. Though we like to imagine that it's just the right size for spiriting a small dog around town—ideally a Pomeranian.
