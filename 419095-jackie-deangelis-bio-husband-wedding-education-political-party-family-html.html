Jackie DeAngelis' education

The business journalist went to Cornell University. She pursued a Bachelor of Arts degree in Asian Studies and History at the university. Career

DeAngelis' career path is nothing short of impressive. She is an experienced technology analyst. She worked for Oaktree Capital Management, and her role at the firm was to identify opportunities for investment in new and emerging markets.

She also worked at CNBC for 13 years. During this period, she held different positions and conducted her duties with utmost professionalism. At the beginning of her CNBC career, she served as the Director of Strategic Programming and Development. She also held an Investing in America event with the former president, Barack Obama, which led to an Emmy nomination.
