Kylie Jenner Bared Her Sideboob in a Backless Red Dress

 Posted on January 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  284 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In case you missed it, sideboob is trending. From Emily Ratajkowski to Florence Pugh and Hailey Bieber, celebrities are freeing the sideboob. In light of her recent modest style, Kylie Jenner, for one, isn't going to be left out. 

On Thursday, the makeup mogul celebrated the launch of her first solo fragrance, Cosmic, with a party in Los Angeles, and for the occasion, she returned to her sexy sartorial roots. Wearing a red bodycon dress with major cutouts, Jenner showed a sliver of her sideboob while completely baring her left shoulder and back. The midi-length dress wrapped around her neck and featured a full-length sleeve on one side, while leaving the other completely naked. She accessorized with white slingback round-toe heels and diamond jewelry.

As for glam, Jenner pulled her dark brunette hair back into a messy updo with face-framing waves and paired a nude glossy lip with a bronzy glow and coordinating eyeshadow.

Jenner's latest ultra-sexy look comes just a week after she addressed speculation that she purposefully began dressing more conservatively as a result of her new relationship with Timothée Chalamet. When asked about the shift in her style during an interview with The New York Times, the reality star didn't offer much information, telling the outlet: "I don’t know how I feel about that. I just don’t want to talk about personal things.”

However, she did credit her 2-year-old son with Travis Scott, Aire, for allowing her to experiment more. "It takes me a while after I have a baby to feel like myself again," Jenner explained. "Then you get your body back, and you're like, 'Wait, the trends have changed.'"
