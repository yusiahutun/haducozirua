The sustainable Christmas gift guide 2023

Haeckels Bladderwrack + Buckthorn Body Wash

Haeckels Bladderwrack + Buckthorn Body Wash

The whole ethos of British brand Haeckels is driven by sustainability, whether that's using natural waste resources in its products, or creating fully recyclable and compostable packaging. The basis for all the brand's skin and bodycare is seaweed, picked by hand in Margate, that's anti-bacterial, skin rebuilding and anti-inflammatory. This particularly lovely body cleanser has a low-foaming formulation and is packed with extracts of indigenous plants – the sleek packaging also looks gorgeous on a bathroom counter.

Completedworks 'Crushed Bubbles' 14kt Gold-Vermeil Earrings

Completedworks 'Crushed Bubbles' 14kt Gold-Vermeil Earrings

We don't know anyone who wouldn't be delighted to find a pair of knockout earrings from brand-of-the-moment Completedworks under the tree this year. These pleasingly chunky earrings are a particular favourite: playfully designed to mimic the look of burst bubble wrap, they're cast in recycled gold and prettily packaged in a recycled cardboard box. Perfect for New Year's Eve parties and beyond.

'Evergreen' Refillable Diary

'Evergreen' Refillable Diary

A fresh Smythson diary is undoubtedly the most luxurious way to signal the start of a new year. For 2024, go for the sustainable option with Smythson's first refillable diary. Designed with a week-to-view layout, the refill pad (in the brand's iconic pale blue, of course) can be slotted into the soft, leather cover year after year, making this the conscious option for the everyday diarist. The cover comes in a range of shades, but we love this dark khaki.

Studio 50 Scented Soy Wax Candle

Studio 50 Scented Soy Wax Candle

A candle may seem like a fairly ubiquitous present, but this stunning dual-wick iteration from Henry Holland Studio is anything but boring. The candle itself is hand-poured and finished in London using the finest soy wax (which burns cleaner and is non-toxic, unlike the usual paraffin wax used to make candles) and scented with a cedar wood base and light, fresh top notes. The handmade ceramic chalice can be cleaned and used as a vase or trinket tray when the candle has burnt down, making this the gift that keeps on giving.

Lambswool Aran Turtleneck

Lambswool Aran Turtleneck

A piece made from high quality lambswool is an excellent investment, particularly when its ethically produced, beautifully designed and luxuriously soft to boot. Moss + Cable, a small, Donegal-based brand that specialises in knitwear famous to the region, sells handmade, unisex cardigans and jumpers that are crafted from 100 per cent lambswool, farmed in the nearby area. When looked after well, they'll last a lifetime.

The Tartan Blanket Co. Neutral Check Undyed Alpaca Blanket

The Tartan Blanket Co. Neutral Check Undyed Alpaca Blanket

Not sure what to get the friend who has everything? A supremely warm and cuddly throw from The Tartan Blanket Co. is a reliable choice, particularly if it's this neutral check version. Made from undyed alpaca wool – a traceable Peruvian yarn, famous for its durability and warmth – combined with recycled nylon, it's beautiful slung over the back of a sofa or laid across fresh, white cotton bedding.

Remember Reusable Glass Straws

Remember Reusable Glass Straws

Now 30% Off

These brightly-hued (and extremely well-priced) glass straws are the ideal stocking filler for any sustainably minded hostess – and they really add flair when stacked in a tumbler on a cocktail trolley, too. Made by German company Remember, which creates unique homewares that hero colour and shape, these straws are crafted from 100 per cent borosilicate glass, and will add a touch of indulgence to any long drink.

'Memory' Bracelet

'Memory' Bracelet

This exceedingly beautiful bracelet from sustainable jewellery brand Kimai is a showstopper of a Christmas present. Featuring a delicate string connecting two diamonds and an 18 carat gold disc, it can be personalised with initials or a special inscription. What's more, the diamonds are lab-grown and the gold is recycled, cutting out environmentally damaging mining processes.

Mother Of Pearl 'Kirsty' Faux Pearl-Embellished Wool-Blend Sweater

Mother Of Pearl 'Kirsty' Faux Pearl-Embellished Wool-Blend Sweater

There's perhaps nothing that's more gratefully received at Christmas than a cosy jumper – especially when it's this oatmeal toned, pearl-encrusted version from Mother of Pearl, which is the very epitome of quiet luxury. Creative director, Amy Powney, has reworked the brand's entire supply chain by introducing natural fibres, transparent manufacturing and a socially responsible approach to suppliers, to perfect the art of designing with a conscience.

Reusable Water Bottle

Reusable Water Bottle

Metal water bottles may be everywhere these days, but none are so chic as Dior's version: made from stainless steel, it showcases the streets of Paris, from Faubourg-Saint-Honoré where Mr Dior glimpsed the lucky star that inspired him to found his design house, to 30 Avenue Montaigne, the iconic maison address and flagship store. Echoing Maria Grazia Chiuri's SS23 ready-to-wear collection, this graphically shaded map design turns an everyday object into a real work of art.

Stella McCartney 'Falabella' Mini Faux-Leather Tote

Stella McCartney 'Falabella' Mini Faux-Leather Tote

Instantly recognisable with its chain trim, Stella McCartney's iconic 'Falabella' tote bag – here, reimagined in more petite proportions – is the ultimate indulgent gift for an eco-conscious fashion fan. Neatly finished with tonal whipstitching, it's made from the brand's signature faux-leather, in line with the label's consistently animal-friendly approach.
