Emily Ratajkowski Wears Faux Fur with Yellow Yeezy Boots in NYC

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  294 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Emily Ratajkowski is keeping the chic maternity looks coming.

Last weekend, the model and soon-to-be mother was photographed grabbing coffee with her husband, Sebastian Bear-McClard, in New York City. For the outing, Ratajkowski wore a tight black knit top; an army green faux-fur coat by Apparis; light-wash, low-rise jeans; and bright-yellow snakeskin boots courtesy of Yeezy's Season 7 collection. The model topped the look off with a black knit beanie and a matching belt, oversized sunglasses, and a protective face mask.

Robert Kamau//Getty Images

Perhaps her best accessory though? Their family dog, Colombo, who adorably tagged along for the java run.

Robert Kamau//Getty Images

On her Instagram recently, Ratajkowski shared an adorable snap of her and her fur baby, this time opting for a white crop top and low-rise jeans, with Colombo sweetly peeping into the frame, ready for his close-up.

"Belly and my first baby Colombo (he fell asleep like this)," wrote the model alongside the cute photo.

Ratajkowski has remained an open book since announcing the news that she was expecting her first child back in October. On a number of occasions, the model bared her baby bump, including another time she was out and about in New York, on Halloween, and even for the occasional sultry selfie, a.k.a. while wearing nothing at all.

The style star broke the baby news with a short video clip shared to Instagram directed by her friend Lena Dunham, which featured the model reciting a spoken-word poem dedicated to her unborn child.
