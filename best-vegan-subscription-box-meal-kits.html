6 best plant-based vegan subscription boxes for 2023

 Posted on March 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  563 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

New Year means the beginning of Veganuary, and from animal welfare to health concerns and environmental issues there are plenty of benefits to having meat-free meals and making the diet swap to veganism.

But, as with any big lifestyle change, it's not an easy feat. Out of those who signed up for Veganuary last year only 61% stuck with a fully-vegan diet for the month. 

Enter vegan meal kits - the stress of food shopping, coming up with meal ideas and cooking can now be a thing of the past. Scroll on to see our favourites...

Mindful Chef vegan meals

The best pick for those on a health kick, Mindful Chef's vegan boxes are made using only the highest quality produce and never include refined carbs and sugars, dairy or gluten. Available from 2-30 January, the recipes on offer change every week and will include the likes of Gnocchi with Smoky Pomodoro Sauce and Heura Plant-based Chick'n & Root Veg Pie. If you're new to Mindful Chef you can get 25% off your first four boxes, and even better? For every meal bought another meal is donated to a child in poverty.

Gousto vegan meals

If it’s good enough for Joe Wicks, it’s good enough for us. And with Gousto’s vegan recipes changing weekly, you’re sure to get a great variety of veggie-filled favourites. Usually all you have to provide yourself is a bit of salt and pepper, something we can all manage. The Thai-Style Coconut Udon Noodles With Crushed Peanuts is next on our list to try!

SHOP NOW

AllPlants vegan meals

Everything on the AllPlants menu is vegan, as the name suggests, so there’s no worry of scouring through ingredient lists just to double-check there’s no sneaky milk or butter hiding away in there somewhere. And these meals don’t require any cooking talent from you, they are already made and frozen ready for a fuss-free dinner in minutes. 

SHOP NOW

Planty vegan meals

Another all-vegan menu comes courtesy of Planty. Choose from a range of delicious chef-prepared meals delivered to your door, which you can heat up straight from the freezer in minutes. We like the sound of the Mac 'No' Cheese and the Mushroom Massaman Curry. For 20% off your first box use the code GOPLANTS.

SHOP NOW

Simply Cook vegan meals

With fifteen vegan recipes including Mie Goreng with Tofu, Iranian Vegetable Stew and Quorn Keema Matar, Simply Cook's letterbox-sized meal kits provide the perfect feast. Included in each kit are four recipe cards and the spices or flavourings needed for each meal. Fresh produce will need to come from your local supermarket, but thanks to the detailed instructions you’ll be in and out in no time.

SHOP NOW

Graze vegan snacks

If it’s snacks you’re after then look no further than Graze for all of your sweet, savoury and everything in-between treats. With the likes of peanut butter chocolate flapjack and a Pina Colada fruit mix, they put that bag of salt and vinegar crisps to shame.
