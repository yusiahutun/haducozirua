It looked as though she had been taking style notes - and not to mention career advice - from Liz Hurley.

On Tuesday night, actress Maggie Q stole the show at the Divergent premiere in a very revealing LBD.

While she is in no way the main star of the film, Maggie turned heads as she arrived at the event held at the Regency Bruin Theatre.

That's one way to get noticed! Maggie Q steals the limelight at Divergent premiere as she wears no underwear in a VERY revealing outfit

In the movie, Maggie plays the minor character Tori, although judging by her outfit, you'd be forgiven for thinking she was the lead role.

Her outfit certainly paid homage to the iconic Versace dress worn by Liz Hurley that catapulted her stardom instantly.

But Maggie wasn't the only one begging for attention at the premiere.

Taking style notes from Liz? The dress appeared to resemble the infamous Versace gown that catapulted Hurley's career

Ashley Judd - who plays the drab mother of the film's heroine - also showed off in a plunging neckline.

Meanwhile Kate Winslet showed off her amazing post-pregnancy figure in a tight-fitting red dress.

The film's star - Shailene Woodley - wore a floor-length golden gown, which had a dramatic edge to it.

Shailene's brunette locks, which she cut into a boyish crop for her upcoming role as a cancer patient in The Fault In Our Stars - appeared perfectly content to be the centre of attention in her unusual gown.

Look at me! Maggie's dress certain demanded attention

Centre of attention: Shailene Woodley grabbed attention in her dramatic slashed to the thigh gown at the Divergent premiere in LA on Tuesday

All grown up: She may play a teenager in the movie but Shailene chose a grown-up gown for the premiere

The brocade dress was a combination of gold and various shades of brown and bronze and featured a stand out draping over one shoulder.

The other shoulder was left bare but some of her back was exposed. 

Shailene added strappy sandals to finish her look.

Fleshbaring: Along with flashing her leg, Shailene's dress was almost backless as she took to the special black carpet at the premiere

Still a kid at heart: She may have looked all grown up in her dress but Shailene couldn't help joking around on the carpet

While the actress is well established thanks to her long-running stint on The Secret Life Of An American Teenager and playing George Clooney's daughter on The Descendants, Shailene is set to hit the big time thanks to Divergent.

At Tuesday's Los Angeles premiere, which took place at the Regency Bruin Theatre in Westwood, Shailene was a far cry from her Divergent character Beatrice 'Tris' Prior.

At the beginning of the movie Tris wears only drab and shapeless grey clothes.

Frenemies: Kate Winslet and Shailene, who play enemies onscreen, looked thrilled to be catching up at the premiere in Los Angeles

Mamma Mia: Despite giving birth to her third child in December, Kate looked fabulous in her long red dress

Paying tribute: Shailene was thrilled to work with Kate and showed her appreciation with a kiss

The movie, which is set in a dystopian futuristic Chicago and based on the novel of the same name, sees people divided into five distinct factions based on their personalities.

However Shailene's character 'Tris' discovers that she is Divergent.

This means she does not fit into any one faction and soon discovers a sinister plot brewing in the seemingly perfect society.

Cosy: Shailene cosied up to her on-screen love interest Theo James, who plays hunky instructor Four

Attention grabbing: While Ashley Judd plays Tris' drab mother in the movie, she made sure she stood out in a dramatic plunging dress at the premiere

Elegant: Shailene and Theo looked elegant in their premiere attire as they posed for pictures on the carpet

And of course, no teen franchise would be complete without a hunky love interest, and in Divergent it comes in the form of Theo James as Four (real name Tobias Eaton).

Brit star Theo, 29, is best known for his brief role in Downton Abbey, playing Turkish diplomat Kemal Pamuk who perished in Mary Crawley's bed, and his part as bullying holiday rep James in The Inbetweeners Movie.

But thanks to his role as the strong but caring Four, he is set to win a whole new legion of fans and Shailene is leading the pack.
