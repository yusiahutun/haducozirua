Samidohs Wife Edday Nderitu Confirms She's Not Coming Back to Kenya

 Posted on October 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  556 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • Edday Nderitu has made it unequivocally clear that she has no intention of reconciling with her husband, Samidoh
  • Edday referred a concerned fan to a previous post, where she candidly shared her reservations about raising their kids in a polygamous setting
  • The mother of three has been having the best moments of her life in the US with their children and she is not planning to return home soon

Mugithi singer Samidoh Muchoki's wife, Edday Nderitu, has hinted at her reluctance to reconcile with her husband.

The revelation came to light during a Facebook interaction with a concerned fan, Kamau Mpesa, who expressed disapproval of Edday's host, Bernice, not advocating for Edday's return to Samidoh.

"I know you will say it's jealousy but I'm not here to praise Bernice. She wants Edday to leave Samidoh. That is very wrong. You can't leave Samidoh because of marrying another wife. It rains everywhere," Kamau stated.

Edday's previous heartfelt post

However, she firmly expressed her unwillingness to subject her children to a polygamous family dynamic, particularly with a woman significantly older than her and lacking moral values and respect.
