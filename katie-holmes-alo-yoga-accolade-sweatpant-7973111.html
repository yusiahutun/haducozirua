Katie Holmes Can't Stop Wearing These Alo Yoga Sweatpants

 Posted on February 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  455 words  |  Aldo Pusey

There are two types of sweatpants: The ones you wear out and about and those that only leave the house if there’s a package waiting outside your door. While every wardrobe needs a few pairs dedicated to lounging at home, I’m taking notes from Katie Holmes’ streetwear looks and adding a pair of perfectly oversized joggers to my fall closet. And not only am I drawing inspiration from her, but I’m investing in the same exact sweatpants she’s been wearing on repeat.

The actress was spotted in Alo Yoga’s Accolade Sweatpant three times last month (in colors white and gray), and she just stepped into them again on September 19, worn with a camel brown cardigan and sneakers for an effortlessly cool errand ’fit. Unfortunately, the bright green shade she sported a few days ago is no longer available on the brand’s site, but the limited-time cosmic grey option is just as good and a fun piece to toss on with neutral tops and hoodies. 

Alo Yoga Accolade Sweatpant

While the Alo Yoga Accolade Sweatpant was put on my radar in July, I’ve been eyeing the brand for years thanks to the looks of celebrities and supermodels like Jennifer Garner, Hailey Bieber, and Kendall Jenner. That’s because the pieces are high quality, comfortable yet cute, and obviously stand the test of time, as we’ve seen the same styles worn over and over again by Hollywood’s fans of the brand. 

The Katie Holmes-worn Alo Yoga joggers top other sweatpants thanks to their relaxed fit that’s casual and flattering at the same time and the blend of French terry, cotton, and polyester that’s “very comfortable” and “holds up great in the wash,” according to shoppers. You can place the elastic waistband higher or lower depending on your vibe, and use the hidden drawstrings to adjust the fit, as we know Holmes does because the strings peek out from her waistline. They come in five signature colors, including the actress’s go-to white and gray, as well as seven limited-time shades. 

Alo Yoga Accolade Sweatpant

Shoppers can’t get enough of the classic joggers, with one describing them as “the right baggy look” and “expensive looking.” Another customer shared that “even though [they’re] dressed down,” they “feel put together,” while a third person said they are the “best sweats [they’ve] ever put on [their] body.” 

Whether you’re running errands, traveling, or walking around a city, the Alo Yoga Accolade Sweatpant will help you look effortlessly cool while staying comfortable. Keep scrolling to see more popular sweatpant styles to add to your athleisure collection.
