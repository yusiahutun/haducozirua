Rihanna &amp; A$AP Rocky Spent Christmas Together in Barbados

 Posted on February 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  287 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It seems that love is in the air this Christmas, as Rihanna's romance with A$AP Rocky is reportedly heating up.

As reported by People, the attractive couple spent Christmas together in Barbados, with the publication confirming that A$AP Rocky arrived on Rihanna's home island on Wednesday.

According to People, "The couple, who wore face masks, was spotted spending Christmas Eve together as they were on their way to a Catamaran sunset cruise while vacationing in her native Barbados." A source told the publication, "Rihanna has been in Barbados since [last] Thursday ... A$AP joined her and they are spending Christmas together with Rihanna's family."

As A$AP spent the holiday with his superstar girlfriend's family, it's safe to assume that their relationship is going pretty well.

BACKGRID

Rumors that Rihanna had started dating A$AP Rocky began in early 2020, following the news that the "Umbrella" singer had split from her boyfriend of three years, Hassan Jameel.

A source spoke to People about Rihanna's new romance with A$AP Rocky and said, "They've been inseparable the past few weeks. It's a new relationship, but they both seem very into it. They've always seemed to have a great time together. They have a lot in common."

The source continued, "They both are very much about helping out in the communities where they grew up A$AP is generous, and so is Rihanna. A$AP is known for being a great guy. Rihanna seems very happy dating A$AP."
