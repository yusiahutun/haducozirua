The best quilted jackets and coats to invest in now

Totême

Totême’s quilted shell jacket is a minimalist's dream. Made from recycled polyester, its details include diamond print stitching all over, piped hems and a collarless neckline.

Burberry

This coat balances vintage with new-season design perfectly. Featuring a corduroy collar, diamond quilting, oversized pockets and a front press stud opening – it doesn't skimp on the details. Wear with jeans and flats for a day out in the city.

Ganni

Ganni

Credit: Courtesy

Made from recycled polyester, this mid-length, diamond quilted jacket from Ganni has a relaxed, oversized fit, making it ideal for layering over jumpers in the winter months.

The Frankie Shop

The Frankie Shop

Credit: Courtesy

You may have rediscovered the quilted trend after The Frankie Shop's Teddy coat went viral a few seasons ago – and now the New York-based brand is back with more quilted styles. This relaxed, slightly longer coat is made from ripstock. It has a boxy fit and a collarless neckline which adds an edge to an otherwise simple design.

Totême

We couldn't resist sneaking another Totême design into this edit. The brand excels in its selection of quilted jackets and we particularly love this camel, collared design.

Avavav

Inject your spring wardrobe with some serious colour by investing in Avavav's patterned shell jacket in purple, which has a real Eighties feel about it. Team with tailored black trousers and heels for an excellent evening look.

Closed
