Your 2024 Tarot Card Horoscope &amp; Future Predictions

 Posted on April 12, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1625 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The new year holds much promise, and your 2024 Tarot horoscope reflects just one of the many opportunities that will arrive as you let go of the past and embrace something new.

2024 is a powerful year for so many reasons. In numerology, each year is bestowed with something called a Universal Year number that signifies what you can look forward to in the new year. This single digit number is the summation of each number within the year, and in the case of 2024, that number is 8, because 2+0+2+4 adds up to 8. In numerology, the number 8 holds much promise, as it is symbolically tied to manifestation, financial expansion, strength, and transformation. You spent a lot of time clearing away blockages and removing yourself from what is no longer meant for you during 2023, as 2+0+2+3 adds up to a 7. In numerology, the Universal Year number of 7 reflects spirituality, reflection, and inner work, which is one reason why 2023 was so damn difficult. Luckily, it paved the way for the blessings that will arrive during 2024.

If you’re looking for further clarity for how 2024 will affect you, the Tarot can provide that and so much more. A deck of Tarot consists of 78 cards, each one brimming with infinite possibilities. The imagery and the symbolism associated with the Major and Minor Arcana bring your attention to something important that you may not be aware of. And because Sasha Purdy—StyleCaster’s resident graphic designer—is currently working on our very own Tarot card deck, there’s no better time to give you a taste of what these cards have in store for you.

Your 2024 Tarot Card Reading For Each Zodiac Sign

Here’s what the main theme of your 2024 experience is looking like, according to a Tarot card pull for your sun sign and/or rising sign:

  • Aries: The Emperor

    You’re stepping into a leadership position, but not just one of power and authority. The Emperor indicates that you’re becoming a provider and a caretaker; someone that people in your social circle and community look up to for guidance. And although this new role will require you to become more demanding and strict, it will also ask you to lead with kindness, harmony, and fairness.     2024 is marked my prosperity and abundance, but especially for you. After all, the 9 of Cups signifies happiness, emotional fulfillment, and contentment. During 2024, you will have everything you need in terms of love, work, relationships, lifestyle, health, and wealth.   • Gemini: Queen of Cups

That’s right—the Queen of Cups signifies that you’re stepping into your role as a lover, a nurturer, and an intuitive healer, but only in the most esteemed sense. This year, you’re becoming someone that others lean on for support in its most tender and heartfelt form. If you’ve always been avoidant or resistant to forming emotional ties, 2024 will be the year your heart strengthens its roots and sustains those around you in a mutually beneficial exchange. The Queen of Cups wants to be there for others because it teaches her how to be there for herself. Read your full yearly 2024 horoscope here.

These changes can manifest in the physical, but they can also unfold in a more spiritual sense as revelations and epiphanies create massive upheaval in your life. All you need to know is that not everything is in your control this year, which is perfectly OK. The Universe is intervening on your plans and removing all the blockages that have been inhibiting your potential. Read your full 2024 yearly horoscope here.

  • Leo: Knight of Pentacles

The Knight of Pentacles signifies that you have a goal in mind and you’ll stop at nothing in order to achieve it. During 2024, you should establish a healthy and efficient routine and stay focused on the task at hand. If you keep practicing, showing up at work, and refining your craft, the end of the year will bring you so much revelation and reward. Read your full 2024 yearly horoscope here.

  • Virgo: Page of Pentacles

    This year, you’re starting a new venture and approaching an exciting new beginning at work. You may have been dreaming about a creative idea or a brilliant plan for many months (or even years) and 2024 is when you’ll finally start the process of getting the ball rolling. The Page of Pentacles is a card of opportunities and manifestation, activating your ability to bring your vision into reality. If you actually work toward your dreams rather than simply talk about it, the end of 2024 could lead to promotions, raises, and new forms of income. Read your full 2024 yearly horoscope here.

  • Libra: The World

    2024 is the year you finally reach the end of a long and arduous journey. You’ve been through so many ups and downs as you try to reach your goal and there may have been moments you felt like giving up. The World card is here to remind you why you never quit when the going got rough, because it signifies that you’re ready to complete this chapter and finish off strong. The World means you’re in the process of reaching a meaningful accomplishment and integrating the lessons each failure and success taught you along the way. Read your full 2024 yearly horoscope here.

  • Scorpio: Queen of Swords

    This year, you’re learning how to communicate your desires, needs, and thoughts in a way that strikes fear into those who dare question you. The Queen of Swords is honest, intellectual, independent, and direct; a figure who never minces words or sugarcoats the truth. In 2024, you’re learning how to speak your boundaries clearly and concisely, thereby allowing you to create your own reality by speaking it into existence. This year, you’re no longer allowing fear to prevent you from saying, doing, or feeling whatever feels authentic to you. Read your full 2024 yearly horoscope here.

  • Sagittarius: 6 of Cups

    This year, you’re going back to the basics; back to the part of your soul that remains innocent and untouched by the darkness of adulthood. The 6 of Cups is tied to your inner child, bringing you back in touch with your family, your roots, and your upbringing. This year, you’re searching for the parts of your soul that represent the core of who you really are. In 2024, you’re striving to reconnect with your authentic self by getting reacquainted with childhood friends, distant relatives, and your hometown.   • Aquarius: King of Wands

  • Pisces: 7 of Swords
