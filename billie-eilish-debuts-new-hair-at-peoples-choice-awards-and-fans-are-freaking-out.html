Billie Eilish debuts 'new hair' at People's Choice Awards and fans are 'freaking out'

 Posted on February 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  439 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Billie Eilish stunned at the People’s Choice Awards as she showed off her new hair, and she’s back to black, (or dark brown depending on the lighting).

Fans had been speculating the music star had ditched her neon roots, as she wore a patterned headscarf at the 96th Oscars Nominees Luncheon. Billie Eilish then took to social media posting a pic with headphones covering her ‘all black’ hair. Now, she’s gone public, and the 22-year-old is officially back!

Billie Eilish debuts ‘new’ hair at People’s Choice Awards

It’s out with the old and in with the new. Or, in Billie’s case, out with the new and in with the old.

In August, the Ocean Eyes singer stunned fans as she debuted her vibrant red roots. Hard launching the new style, the singer captioned the post “Remember me?” on social media.

It wasn’t the first time Eilish had added a neon pop to her hair, famously sporting neon green in her locks during the Bad Guy era. Although fans loved the color, they loved her going back to basics just as much.

The star came out on the carpet, flicking her hair, and was met by a huge round of cheers from fans. It looks like social media was also adopting the same energy.

“SHE LOOKS SO GOOD OH MY GODDDDDDDDDD,” exclaimed one.

“Prettiest girl ever,” a second said.

One confused fan exclaimed: “I CAN’T TELL WHETHER IT’S BROWN OR BLACK!!”

Whatever color you want to call it, she looks great either way.

The star goes ‘viral’ for conversation with Kylie Minogue

As she took home the TV Performance of the Year for her work in the series Swarm, had all eyes on her for several reasons at the show. One of these was a ‘juicy’ conversation with Kylie Minogue.

The duo we never knew we needed were seen having a secret conversation, as Billie covered her mouth with her hand at one point, before pointing over with her thumb. She must have learned from Selena Gomez’s ‘mishap’ at the Golden Globes.

However, social media detectives listened closely to the audio, in which she seemingly said “I’m like, there’s some like, TikTokers here, which is just… don’t need it.”

Fans seemed to be on the star’s side, as one wrote: “Billie Eilish talking about the plague of TikTokers is hilarious.”

“There are actually some talented TikTokers but idk why they invite the bad ones lmaoo,” tweeted another.

“She’s so unintentionally funny,” another claimed.

Amongst the TikTokers was Youtuber and beauty influencer James Charles, and it seemed like fans were on the same side as Billie supposedly was.
