About

Raye Dowell is a 62 year old American Actress. Born on 22nd August, 1961 in United States, she is famous for Malcolm X (1992). Her zodiac sign is Leo.

Forest Whitaker is a 62 year old American Actor. Born Forest Steven Whitaker III on 15th July, 1961 in Longview, Texas, USA, he is famous for his various roles in film and television, such as Bumpy Johnson in the Epix crime drama series Godfather of Harlem, Idi Amin in the 2006 historical drama film The Last King of Scotland, Private Edward Garlick in the 1987 war comedy film Good Morning, Vietnam, Ghost Dog in the 1999 martial arts crime thriller drama film Ghost Dog: The Way of the Samurai, Burnham in the 2002 thriller film Panic Room, Captain Ed Ramey in the 2002 psychological thriller film Phone Booth, and Saw Gerrera in the 2016 epic space opera film Rogue One: A Star Wars Story in a career that spans 1981–present. His zodiac sign is Cancer.
