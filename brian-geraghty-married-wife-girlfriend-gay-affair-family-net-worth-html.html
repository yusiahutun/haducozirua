Career and Professional Life

As a TV actor, Brain Geraghty started his career with a short role in legal drama ‘Law and Order’ in 1999 and an episode of Crime drama series ‘The Sopranos.’ The brain then worked on some of the best series in TV namely ‘True Blood’ and ‘Boardwalk Empire.’ But Brian is best remembered for his role as ‘Officer Sean Roman’ in the Chicago PD series.

As a Film artist, Geraghty started with a small role as a cop in ‘Petty Thief.’  He then worked in many of the feature films like ‘Cruel world,’ ‘Jarhead,’ ‘The Guardian,’  ‘The Hurt Locker,’ ‘The Chameleon,’ ‘Open House,’ lastly seen in ‘Wild-like.’ Brian is known for his role as Specialist Owen Eldridge in Oscar and Academy Award Winning film, ‘Hurt locker.’
