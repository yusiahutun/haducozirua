Kelly Clarkson, Migos, Taylor Swift

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  317 words  |  Martina Birk

Another work week is coming to a close, which means another wave of new music has arrived and TooFab has found 10 artists to consider listening to while you're waiting to clock out.

On the album front, Kelly Clarkson released her eighth studio record, "Meaning Of Life," and Ty Dolla $ign dropped his second studio album, "Beach House 3."

Taylor Swift debuted the music video for "...Ready For It?" early Friday morning, and although the video's director, Joseph Kahn, insists the video for Swift's second single off her upcoming album is an "homage to sci-fi and anime," it attracted an army of haters calling it out for eerie similarities to "Ghost in the Shell" and cultural appropriation. Watch it below to weigh in on the debate on your own Twitter feed.

There were also a bunch of great collaborations this week, including Selena Gomez and Marshmello teaming up on "Wolves," Clean Bandit and Julia Michaels on "I Miss You," Pitbull and Fifth Harmony on "Por Favor," Kygo and John Newman on "Never Let You Go," and Migos, Nicki Minaj and Cardi B on "Motor Sport."

Sam Smith dropped new single "Burning" and Scottish rock band Franz Ferdinand dropped synth-heavy new track "Always Ascending."
