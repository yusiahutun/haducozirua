The stars will star in the play alongside Now You See Me star Jesse Eisenberg, The Big Bang Theory star Kunal Nayyar and Billions star Annapurma Sriram, who all starred in the original off-Broadway production in New York.

The play, written by Eisenberg, runs in London from 27 May to 13 August 2016.

It tells the story of Ben, who’s a bully. When he discovers his school crush is marrying a straight-laced banker, he decides to destroy their relationship and win her back.

Alfie takes on the role of banker Ted, and will make his West End debut in the production.

The actor is used to playing a bullied man after becoming a household name thanks to his role as the systematically abused Theon Greyjoy in Game Of Thrones.
