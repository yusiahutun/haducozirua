Kourtney Kardashian dresses like character from True Romance for date night with Travis Barker

 Posted on April 21, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1012 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker have a shared love of 1993 film True Romance, often referencing lines from the movie in their loved-up Instagram posts. 

Last week, the couple enjoyed a romantic beach movie night in Mexico, with the reality star, 42, dressing similar to Patricia Arquette's character Alabama Whitman.

The outing comes after Travis' ex-wife Shanna Shanna Moakler accused the Blink 182 drummer of copying their relationship for romantic moments with his new girlfriend, with True Romance having been the theme of their 2004 wedding.
