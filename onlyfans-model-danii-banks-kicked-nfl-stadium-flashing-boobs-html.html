OnlyFans model Danii Banks is kicked out of NFL stadium for flashing her boobs

 Posted on February 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  413 words  |  Martina Birk

An OnlyFans model was kicked out of a stadium during an NFL game for flashing her boobs.

Danii Banks, 33, recorded herself flashing her boobs at a Las Vegas Raiders game last Sunday night during their game against the New York Jets.

Security guards and police officers spotted Banks, and immediately asked her to leave the stadium. 

The OnlyFans model told TMZ that the security guards and police officers did not escort her out, but did tell her that she 'didn't have to go home, but she had to get the hell outta Sunday night's game.'

The police officers did not give Banks a ticket or arrest her after the incident, no criminal charges have been filed against her.

The model took a video of herself flashing the crowd and posted it on her social media after the game was over

Banks is a porn star famous on social media for her curvy swimsuit pictures, and currently has 8 million followers on Instagram.

She's also in a partnership with Sculpt NYC - a business that provides services such as body and face sculpting, glass skin and red carpet facials, and laser hair removal.

The Sculpt NYC brand recently began working in Las Vegas, where Banks lives, offering other services such as Botox and facial contouring.

Banks has accused former Las Vegas Raider Damon Arnette of stealing jewelry and money from her

The longtime model and Fashion Nova retail company ambassador has a previous history with the Las Vegas Raiders.

Banks accused former Las Vegas Raider Damon Arnette on an episode of Pillow Talk podcast last August of stealing her Cartier watch, phone, and money.

'Damon Arnette, the little football player, that played for the Raiders that got kicked off trying to shoot someone or some s***,' Banks said.

She continued: 'Well, I went to the bathroom, stole my f****** bust down Cartier watch, took my other phone and Zelle'd them all the money out of my account.'

'I have it all on camera. Him and his friends leaving my house. Come to find out he does that to b****** and then f****** did it to me.'

Arnette responded to the accusations on his Instagram Story right after she appeared on the podcast, and said that Banks should 'figure out a better way' to deal with him, or 'don't deal with' him.
