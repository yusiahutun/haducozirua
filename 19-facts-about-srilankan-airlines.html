Srilankan Airlines was founded in 1979.

Established as Air Lanka, the airline commenced operations on 1 September 1979 and rebranded as Srilankan Airlines in 1998.

Srilankan Airlines is headquartered in Colombo.

The airline is based in the capital city of Sri Lanka, Colombo, and operates its main hub at Bandaranaike International Airport.

It is a member of the oneworld alliance.

Srilankan Airlines became a member of the prestigious oneworld alliance in 2014, allowing it to offer seamless connectivity to over 1,000 destinations worldwide.

Srilankan Airlines has a fleet of modern aircraft.

With a fleet of Airbus A320, A321neo, A330, and A350 aircraft, Srilankan Airlines ensures a comfortable and safe journey for its passengers.

The airline has won several awards.

Srilankan Airlines has been recognized globally for its exceptional service and performance, receiving accolades such as the Best Airline in South Asia and Best Airline Staff in South Asia.

Srilankan Airlines offers a wide range of destinations.

With its extensive route network, the airline connects passengers to numerous destinations across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and Australia.

It provides a unique onboard dining experience.

Srilankan Airlines offers a delectable selection of meals inspired by Sri Lankan and international cuisines, ensuring a memorable dining experience at 35,000 feet.

Srilankan Airlines focuses on sustainability.

The airline takes environmental responsibility seriously, implementing various initiatives to reduce its carbon footprint and promote sustainable practices.

Srilankan Airlines offers a comprehensive frequent flyer program.

The FlySmiLes program allows loyal travelers to earn miles and enjoy exclusive benefits, including upgrades, lounge access, and priority services.

Srilankan Airlines ensures passenger safety and comfort.

With stringent safety measures and attentive cabin crew, Srilankan Airlines prioritizes the well-being of its passengers throughout their journey.

It provides a range of travel classes.

From Economy Class to Business Class, Srilankan Airlines offers a variety of travel options to cater to different passenger preferences and budgets.

Srilankan Airlines operates a dedicated cargo service.

In addition to passenger flights, the airline operates an efficient cargo service, facilitating the transportation of goods across the globe.

It has codeshare agreements with major airlines.

Srilankan Airlines has established codeshare partnerships with renowned airlines such as Emirates, Qatar Airways, Malaysia Airlines, and British Airways, expanding its connectivity.

The airline promotes Sri Lanka’s rich culture.

By showcasing Sri Lanka’s vibrant heritage through its in-flight entertainment and onboard amenities, Srilankan Airlines offers passengers a glimpse into the country’s diverse traditions.

The airline’s dedicated customer service team ensures that passengers receive prompt assistance and support throughout their travel journey.

Srilankan Airlines supports charitable initiatives.

The airline actively contributes to community development and social causes through various corporate social responsibility programs.

It has a strong commitment to aviation safety.

Srilankan Airlines adheres to international safety standards and continuously invests in training its crew and maintaining its aircraft to ensure the highest level of safety.

Srilankan Airlines embraces technology.

The airline leverages advanced technology and digital platforms to enhance the passenger experience, making it convenient for travelers to book flights and manage their reservations.
