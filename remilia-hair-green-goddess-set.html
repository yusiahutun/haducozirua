Remilia Hairs Green Goddess Set Transformed My Locks After One Wash

My shower is lined with up to 15 products at a time (no joke), and while that’s partly due to my job as a beauty editor, it’s also because I don’t mess around when it comes to keeping my locks in top shape. Don’t get it twisted—while I like options, I am incredibly picky about formulas. That’s why I knew I had to share the latest duo to earn a regular spot in my line-up.

I’ve been a huge fan of Remilia ever since I tried the brand’s Cosmocap, a restorative hair treatment that transforms your hair into silk in minutes—trust me, I wouldn’t use it several days a week if it didn’t. Once I heard Remilia had launched the Green Goddess Set , a matching shampoo and conditioner, I knew it was going to be chefs kiss. 

Related Stories

I wasn’t wrong. A little bit of the shampoo goes a long way, and best of all, it won’t leave your hair feeling like straw after you rinse it out (you know the feeling). The conditioner melts into your hair upon contact and leaves your strands with little to no frizz, even if you air dry after showering. After using both for the first time, my hair literally felt like butter and had the most shine I’ve seen in months. And trust, the results only get better with continued use.

The hair and scalp strengthening formulas were made for those with dry, damaged hair, but if you struggle with achieving volume, this is just as much for you. Coconut oil, avocado oil and rosemary oil work in tandem to instill shine, lift and smoothness to all hair types, including those that are color treated (for reference, my hair is bleached). Both products also contain green tea extract to fortify your roots. 

Amazon shoppers have also shared their love for the shampoo and conditioner in the duo’s reviews section. As one person wrote, “This pair is to die for. They smell like the typical Remilia Hair products (which I hope they come out with a hair perfume), however they’re not overpowering on scent. The best is how gentle but effective the shampoo is at cleansing, and the conditioner is so good my hair soaked it right in. The results talk by themselves as you can see in my picture.”

“This is such a great product for my dry scalp, it cleans my hair so well and gives it a nice shine,” shared another. “I have been using this product for about a week now and I already see a difference in scalp health. I also feel like this product is hydrating and doesn’t make my hair oily like many others. Recommend all the way!”

The Green Goddess Set is available at Amazon for $40 , but you can also purchase the shampoo and conditioner separately at the brand’s storefront.
