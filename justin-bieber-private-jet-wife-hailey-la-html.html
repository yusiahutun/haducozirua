Justin Bieber and his wife Hailey continued with their jet-setting ways by catching a private plane on Friday.

But beforehand, the couple spent the day taking care of a few errands around the Westwood neighborhood of Los Angeles.

The pop superstar, 29, drove around the city in his sleek black Range Rover with Hailey in the front passenger's seat.

For their travel day, Bieber showed R&B singer Ashanti some love by rocking a 'Foolish' T-shirt — the first hit single off her self-titled first studio album.

He styled the baggy tee with groovy floral shorts from from his clothing brand Drew House.

Justin, 29, completed the sporty look with white sneakers and a dayglow beanie cap.

Hailey also opted for comfort as she modeled tight black leggings and a black jacket over a matching T-shirt. 

The Rhode Beauty founder, 27, also wore black sneakers and stylish dark sunglasses during their pre-flight errand run.

Rounding out her overall look, the fashionista had her dark brown locks pulled back off her face and into a ponytail with a part in the middle.

It appears, during all their hustle and bustle, the couple made a pitstop to get a cold drink from a cafe.

In the aftermath, when they made another stop, Hailey stepped out of their vehicle holding what appears to have been an ice coffee or another kind of fancy cold coffee drink.

Eventually, their day out together took them to Van Nuys Airport, which is a smaller public airport in the Van Nuys neighborhood of Los Angeles. 

The pair gathered some of their things and went inside to get ready for their flight to an undisclosed destination. 

Beforehand, the pop superstar, 29, drove around the city in his sleek black Range Rover with Hailey in the front passenger's seat. 

The couple, who have been married for more than five years, were seen on another errand run in the Studio City neighborhood of Los Angeles on Thursday, the day before flying out of Van Nuys Airport on a private jet

Justin and Hailey first met in 2009 backstage at a Today show event, when she was just 12 and he was already a big pop star at 15. 

It turns out her father, actor Stephen Baldwin and Justin's mother, Pattie Mallette, arranged the meeting ahead of time, according to Complex.

Some five years after that initial meeting the two stars started dating, which ended up lasting about two years until their 'dramatic' breakup.

The pair would rekindle their romance and begin dating again in 2018, which resulted in them heading to a New York City courthouse to tie-the-knot in September of that year. 

They got married in front of their friends and family in a more formal wedding a year later at the luxury waterfront resort Montage Palmetto Bluff in South Carolina.
