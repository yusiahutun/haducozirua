Philomena Njeri's Mother Pens Moving Tribute: &quot;My only Child, It Hurts You're Gone&quot;

 Posted on January 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  464 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • Margaret Wanjiru is yet to come to terms with the death of her daughter Philomena Njeri
  • Njeri was shot three times by her husband Jonathan Mukundi before using the same weapon to take his own life
  • Njeri and Mukundi were married for 10 years but had no child
  • Mukundi will be buried to rest later this week at his parents' home in Kiambu

Losing a child is the most painful experience a parent can ever go through. Margaret Wanjiru, the mother of slain Philomena Njeri is heartbroken.

Njeri was killed by her husband Jonathan Mukundi before taking his own life at their Kirigiti home in Kiambu county on August 2, 2021.

Njeri, who was said to be pregnant at the time, had been separated from her husband for over three months and died the night after they reconciled.

She was laid to rest on Wednesday at her parents' home in Kianjege village in Kirinyaga county.

Family and friends paid a glowing tribute to Njeri, and so did her grieving mother Wanjiru.

"I'm jealous of heaven for it has gained an angel of my precious princess. I cried when you passed on. I have cried every day since then. Although I loved you so dearly, it was beyond me to make you stay any longer," Wanjiru said in her tribute, which was read by Njeri's cousin during the burial.

She described Njeri as a woman with a golden heart, saying she had left a void in her heart that will never be filled by anyone.

Njeri the only child

"A daughter is a mother's best friend. Njeri, my one and only child, it hurts me that you are gone but I'm thankful for the best 30-years of my life that you gave me," Wanjiru said.

Adding:

"Through it all, you have always been there. It has always been, me and you against it all. You are my source of joy, my pillar of strength when I thought I couldn't make it."

Facts about Jonathan Mukundi and Philomena Njeri

Jonathan Mukundi and Philomena lived a perfect life that their peers admired. But behind the social media glamorous photos, all was not well at home.

According to sources, Njeri and Mukundi's marriage hit rock bottom recently, and they separated for three months and were not living together.

They reunited on Monday, August 2, had a meeting to try to salvage their marriage.

The 42-year-old tycoon was deeply in love with his wife before things went south. Photos from their social handles prove so.
