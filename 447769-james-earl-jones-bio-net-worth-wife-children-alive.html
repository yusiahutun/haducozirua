James Earl Jones' biography

James Earl Jones is an American actor born in Arkabutla, Mississippi, on January 17, 1931. He is 91 years old as of 2022, and his zodiac is Capricorn. His father, Robert Earl Jones (1910 – 2006), was a boxer, butler, and chauffeur, while his mother, Ruth Williams (1911 – 1986), was a teacher and maid.

Both his parents were of mixed African-American (Irish and native American ancestry). The star has a sibling named Mathew Earl Jones. Shortly after James’ birth, his father left the family and went to be a stage and screen actor in New York and Hollywood. As a result, James never got to know his father until later on in his life after their reconciliation in the 1950s.

When he was five years old, he went to live with his maternal grandparents John Henry and Maggie Connolly. He was raised at their farm in Jackson, Michigan. The grandparents initially lived in Mississippi but were forced to move during the Great Migration.

The transition to living with the grandparents turned out to be traumatic for James; hence he developed severe stuttering. He attended the Browning School before moving to the Dickson Rural Agricultural School, from where he graduated as vice president of his class.

Thanks to his gift for writing poetry, his English teacher encouraged him to speak by reading poetry aloud in class, and he was able to overcome the disorder. He went to the University of Michigan, initially a pre-med major but changed to drama and graduated in 1955.

Career

Before graduating from college, James joined the Reserve Officers' Training Corps, where he excelled. He enjoyed working with fellow cadets in the Pershing Rifles Drill Team and Scabbard and Blade Honor Society. As the Korean War intensified, he waited to be deployed upon receiving his commission as a second lieutenant.

While James waited for his orders, he started to work as a part-time stage crew hand at the Ramsdell Theatre in Manistee, Michigan. Finally, he was commissioned in mid-1953, but the Korean War had ended, so he had to report to Fort Benning for the Infantry Officers Basic Course. He was then assigned to Headquarters and Headquarters Company, 38th Regimental Combat Team.

James made his debut at the Ramsdell Theatre in Manistee as an actor. He started as a stage carpenter in 1953, and two years later, he became an actor and stage manager. His first roles included Shakespeare’s Othello and an appearance in the ABC radio anthology series Theatre-Five.

His Broadway debut came as an understudy to Lloyd Richards in a play titled The Egghead. He then appeared in Sunrise at Campobello as Edward the butler. He continued to act in various works by Shakespeare till the mid-1960s and established himself as one of the best known Shakespearean actors of the time.

He made his film debut in Dr Strangelove or: How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb (1964). He later landed the role of a surgeon and Haitian rebel leader in The Comedians. In 1967 he teamed up with Jane Alexander as they starred in The Great White Hope.

Wife and children

James Earl Jones' first marriage saw him marry American actress and singer Julienne Marie. The two met when James was performing as Othello in 1964. They did not have any children, and they got divorced in 1972.

James remained single for a decade before getting married to actress Cecilia Hart in 1982. In the same year, the couple was blessed with a son they named Flynn. Unfortunately, Hart passed away from ovarian cancer on October 16, 2016.

Awards and net worth

The success of James Earl Jones as an actor cannot be quantified without mentioning his awards. He received the Tony Award for Best Actor in a Play in 1969. He also received the same award in 1987 for his role in Great American Documents.

In 1990 he won the Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series for the series Gabriel’s Fire and a Primetime Emmy Award for Supporting Actor in a Miniseries. In 2011 he was given an Honorary Academy Award.

Earl became a recipient of EGOT (Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, Tony) awards without winning a competitive Oscar. According to Celebrity Net Worth, he has a net worth of $40 million.

Is James Earl Jones still alive?

Yes. In 2015, some people believed that he had passed away after a CNN article titled, No, James Earl Jones Is Not Dead was published. The American actor was also a victim of cyberbullying that surrounded his alleged death.

What is Jones doing today? He continues to work in the film industry. He is a voice actor for various Star Wars projects, including the much-maligned Star Wars Episode 9: The Rise Of Skywalker and the live-action Lion King remake.
