Jalen Slawson Career

Jalen began his career path as a college basketball player. He was named the Area Player of the Year in 2018. During the season, he averaged 14.6 points, 10.5 rebounds, 4.1 blocks, and 3.5 assists per game as a senior. 

2018-19 Season

He played as a reserve player in 26 games. As a freshman, he grabbed 36.4% (FG%), a season-high seven rebounds in a win over North Greenville, and totaled a season-best four points in a victory versus Southern Wesleyan.

2019-20 Season

He played as a sophomore in 32 games and averaged 6.9 points, 5.1 rebounds, 35 steals, 49.7% (FG%), 26 blocked shots, and 1.5 assists per game. He scored a career-high 17 points. 

2020-21 Season

He played as a Junior in 25 games and made 17 game starts. He averaged 8.7 points, 5.3 rebounds, 2.8 assists, 56.9% (FG%), 1.2 steals, and team-best 1.2 blocks per game.

2021-22 Season

He played as a Senior in 34 games. He averaged 14.5 points, 7.4 rebounds, 3.7 assists, 48.8% (FG%), and 1.7 blocks.
