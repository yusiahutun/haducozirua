Karol G shakes off terrifying mid-air emergency drama as she hits red carpet in glamorous frock at 2

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1362 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Karol G put on a dazzling display in an eye-catching gown as she attended the 2024 Billboard Women in Music event in Los Angeles on Wednesday. 

The Columbian songstress, 33, joined other celebrities on the red carpet at the YouTube Theater at Hollywood Park - just days after experiencing an emergency landing due to reports of smoke inside the cockpit of her private jet. 

Despite the distressing incident, the QLONA hitmaker - who was named Billboard's Woman of the Year - flashed a cheerful smile upon arriving to the ceremony. 

She showed off her figure in a fitted, gold sequin Zuhair Murad dress that contained a deep, plunging neckline on the front for a sultry flare. 

The material of the ensemble, including around the neckline, also had numerous rhinestone embellishments that sparkled under the bright lights.

The gown was held up with a halter-styled neckline as well as glittering straps that wrapped around her upper back. 

The beauty - whose real name is Carolina Giraldo Navarro - slipped into a pair of open-toed, silver heels. 

Her long locks were parted in the middle and pulled back into a half up do, allowing the strands to flow down past her shoulders in light waves. 

Karol G opted for minimal accessories to allow her outfit to be the main focal point, and added silver rings on each of her hands, as well as a green and pink bracelet on her left wrist. 

Her makeup was glammed up and comprised of voluminous lashes as well as a shimmering, gold shadow around her eyes. 

A light pink blush and warm contour was added to better accentuate her cheekbones, while a glossy nude-colored tint was worn on her lips for a finishing touch. 

The singer was seen striking a variety of poses to better flaunt her look before the festivities officially commenced. 

While the event was held on Wednesday, fans and viewers will have a chance to watch the ceremony via streaming the following day on Thursday, March 7.

Karol G was also recently announced to be the recipient of Billboard's 2024 Women of the Year award. 

And while on the red carpet for the event, she gushed to Access Hollywood about being the first music artist who records only in Spanish to receive the honor. 'First of all, I'm having so much fun.' 

'I'm seeing a lot of girls that I admire, and I respect so I'm super happy and I feel so proud to be here and to have the honor. To be the first Latina to be Women of the Year, oh my God it's crazy!' 

And while recently talking to Billboard, the performer said, 'It's so meaningful and inspiring to get an award that's not only "woman of this or that category" but Woman of the Year.' 

She also talked about being a global sensation and expressed, 'I started this year with a completely different mindset.' 

'Although this may sound like a beauty queen reply, the place I'm at right now is one of huge responsibility, and it demands that I'm very aware of what surrounds me so I don't make missteps.' 

She later added, 'When I take the stage in a stadium, one of the reasons I cry is because I know one day this will all be over; I'll be home remembering the time I was No. 1. That's life.' 

'But what I will have is a school I built, or a project we launched,' Karol G said, referencing to her Con Cora Foundation launched in 2021. 'Today, and in 10 years and in 50 years, lives will change thanks to something we built.'

The foundation 'was established to continue Karol G's social work and mission to support women in vulnerable conditions,' per the official website.  

Billboard's editorial director, Hannah Karp, opened up about the Columbian songstress receiving the Women of the Year honor in a statement. 

'With her immense talent, Karol G has created a movement for women across the globe due to her empowering lyrics and inspiring confidence.' 

'The release of her groundbreaking album Mañana Será Bonito has proven her a force to be reckoned with in both the English and Spanish charts. We are so excited to honor her as Woman of the Year,' Karp added. 

Last month in February, the star won the Grammy award for Best Música Urbana Album for Mañana Será Bonito. 

She is also currently in the midst of her Mañana Será Bonito tour which kicked off in August 2023. 

On Tuesday, Karol G performed in El Salvador and following the Billboard event, she will perform in Costa Rica for a sold-out show on March 9.

Her appearance at Wednesday's ceremony comes shortly after the superstar singer was pictured in extreme distress as her private jet made an emergency landing in Los Angeles earlier last week. 

The tense moments as the Gulfstream G400 touched down in Van Nuys Airport were caught on camera. The jet was forced into the landing after there was a report of smoke in the cockpit. 

Karol G was seen getting off the plane at Van Nuys Airport after what was termed a 'mid-air emergency.' 

According to ABC7, passengers were seen hugging after they made it back to the ground.

The singer was flying in her private plane, which is decorated with the barbed-wire heart tattoo she has on her arm.

The plane took off from Burbank Airport Thursday with 16 passengers and crew members on board.

No details about the flight are known yet other than that it was 'flying east.' The pilot reported the smoke problem as the aircraft reached the Cajon Pass, about 70 miles east.

The plane made a smooth landing at around 9pm local time at Van Nuys Airport, about 15 miles west of where they took off.

According to the aircraft's tag, it was rented to the singer by VICTOR, a private jet specialist with locations across the world.

A profile on the plane details that it holds 14 people and was manufactured in 2004. It can reach a maximum speed of 550 miles per hour. Its home base is in the Dominican Republic.

In February 2021, Navarro announced in an Instagram post that she had bought a private jet, a Dassault Falcon 2000 which has capacity for ten passengers, from fellow Colombian sensation J Balvin as a birthday gift to herself.

That plane was not the same plane that was involved in the incident that occurred last week.
