Devin McCourty is an American professional soccer player known primarily as the free security for the New England Patriots of the National Football League (NFL).

The NFL defensive back is also known for helping his team win the Super Bowls XLIX and LI. Although he currently plays in free safety, Patriot captain Devin McCourty is equally good at cornerback and kickoff return.

He is one of America’s best football players, who has made the country’s football league more interesting through his technical skills and perfect tactical abilities.

Devin McCourty Bio, Career

Devin McCourty was born in Nyack, New York on August 13, 1987, and grew up with his twin brother Jason McCourty at his mother Phyllis Harrell’s side, while her father Calvin McCourty died at the age of three.

With the help and support of their hard-working mother, Devin and his brother went to St. Joseph’s High School in Montvale, which happens to be one of the best all-boy schools in New Jersey. There Devin became interested in the school and developed his skills as an athlete. In high school, he played cornerback and free safety and was an all-league team in his last two seasons.

He also played basketball as a point guard. As a result, he was included in the All-League squad and was one of the few Rivals.com rated as a two-star recruit.

As a high school student, he scored 50 tackles, three interceptions and three blocked kicks in special teams. This made him the first-team All-Big East in Rutgers in 2009 before he was voted 27th overall in the 2010 NFL Draft.

During his first year of college, Devin retired and played in 13 games, scoring a total of 38 tackles and two interceptions. He also scored 25.4 yards on kickoff returns and had a 98-yard kickoff return, the third-longest in school history. With a career-high of 80 tackles and 10 pass shots, he was appointed to the first team of All-Big East.

Devin McCourty began his professional NFL career in 2010 with the New England Patriots after signing a five-year contract with the team on July 28, 2010. The 2010 NFL Draft saw another great achievement for him as he was voted 27th overall in the first round by the New England Patriots. He was celebrated as the first Team All-Pro by Sporting News and the second Team All-Pro by The Associated Press.

In 2015 McCourty won the Super Bowl XLIX with the Patriots against the Seattle Seahawks (28-24). This was after he made an interception on the Ravens’ quarterback, Joe Flacco, in the AFC Divisional Playoffs game against the Baltimore Ravens. As proof of his tactical skills on the football field, McCourty was named co-captain for the fifth consecutive year. In December 2017 he was named Pro Bowl 2017 and received this honor for the second time.

Devin McCourty Brother

Devin McCourty has a twin brother, Jason McCourty, who is currently playing as a cornerback for the Tennessee Titans. Interesting about the McCourty twins is that they were born 27 minutes apart, with Devin being the older brother. The twins also began their football career at St. Joseph’s High School in Montvale, where they started as a cornerback in 2007.

Jason is also an NFL star, although he will have much success in his career as his brother. While Jason has never been to the playoffs, Devin is about to play his second Super Bowl. Nevertheless, the two brothers have remained close friends, helping and supporting each other in their careers.

The McCourty twins are one of the 12 pairs of identical twins in NFL history. They are also one of the three active pairs of twins in the NFL. Others are Mike and Maurkice Pouncey; Brandon and Brian Dixon. There are only a few ways to distinguish between Devin and his brother Jason. The first is their birthmark. While Jason has a birthmark on his neck and wrist, his older brother Devin has only one birthmark on his ear. Again Devin has a chickenpox scar on his nose, while Jason has a scar on his forehead.

In May 2017, Jason signed a two-year, $6 million contract with the Cleveland Browns. Both Devin and Jason are well-known philanthropists. They have both dedicated their time and wealth to sickle cell research, as several members of their family are affected by the disease, including their father.
