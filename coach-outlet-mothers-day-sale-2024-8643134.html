Mini Chain Wallet

This 60-percent off wallet-bag just reignited my love for tiny accessories. The bag is really small—I mean, it’s literally a wallet on a crossbody chain—but sometimes that’s exactly what you need. “It's small but perfect for a night out,” raved one fan who said it holds “credit cards and car keys or lipstick” while others added that it’s great for concerts and game days. Mollie 25 Tote Bag

On the other end of the spectrum is the Mollie Tote Bag, which shoppers have described as “very spacious” without being comically large. Anna Foldover Clutch Crossbody Bag
