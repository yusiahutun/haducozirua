Juno Temple Agent Provocateur campaign

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  347 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Given that Juno Temple has over 80 sets of Agent Provocateur lingerie, it comes as no surprise that the actress should star in the brand's new campaign.

She fronts the label's Christmas advert, appearing in a new film that aims to show a woman's duality and their naughty or nice side.

"I have loved Agent Provocateur since I was 16," Temple told us. "To me it is feminine lingerie with an edge; it is truly designed for women and in my opinion should be worn under everything –from sweat pants to ball gowns. It gives me a confidence which is mine and makes me feel good about myself. I think Agent Provocateur creates lingerie that is like artwork, but is truly wearable at the same time. Honestly, it's the one thing I can depend on everyday that I know makes me feel good."

[youtube ]https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_qjiRMLf-Gk&feature=youtu.be[/youtube]

Along with a traditional film, the brand has launched a first-of-its-kind video in which viewers can switch between Temple's two alter-egos by tapping the screen or pressing the "n" button on the keyboard. Each tap brings up a different scenario.

For Temple, good underwear is a fast-track to self-empowerment.

"Lingerie should be picked by you for you, and it can really make you feel confident about your body without another human validating you, which I do not take for granted today," she explained. "I really do feel that women's bodies, no matter what shape or size, are one of this planet's great beauties and I think it's almost like picking out jewellery. It should be that feeling, but no one has to see it. It makes me feel excited about my body and I treasure that."

So according to Temple, clearly a lingerie aficionado, what makes the perfect set of lingerie?

"The fabric against your skin," she says. "And the feeling it gives you when you are in pyjamas and a hoodie, but underneath you are wrapped in your delicious secret."

Previous Agent Provocateur campaign stars have included Kate Moss, Abbey Lee Kershaw and Josephine de la Baume.
