Victory: Kelly Clarkson Wins Montana Ranch After Court Upholds Prenup

 Posted on April 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  401 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • On June 4 last year, Kelly Clarkson filed for divorce ending her seven-year marriage to Brandon Blackstock
  • The singer cited irreconcilable differences as her reason for ending her marriage
  • The two have been married since October 2013 and have two children together

Kelly Clarkson has obtained a significant win in her divorce case against her ex-husband, Brandon Blackstock.

The 38-year-old American singer filed for divorce from Brandon Blackstock last year in June, citing irreconcilable differences.

Clarkson and Blackstock have been married for seven years and have two children together: River Rose and Remington Alexander.

Clarkson said that both she and her estranged husband deserve the chance to create a new life, which can only happen when the two are officially divorced.

Clarkson keeps Montana Ranch

According to Fox News, the 'breakaway' hitmaker has been fighting in court to split the accumulated properties, including the Montana Ranch where he is currently living.

But a judge upheld her previous prenuptial agreement with Blackstock. The award-winning singer said she plans to sell off the ranch saying it had 'become a financial burden'.

The two have been married since October 2013.

According to data compiled by Nielsen Music, Clarkson has sold over 25 million albums and 45 million singles globally.

In August last year, the singer also filed a request to restore her maiden name in the middle of a heavily contested divorce. She had previously taken the name Blackstock upon their marriage in 2013.

Clarkson was ordered to pay KSh 21.7 million

In a related story, TUKO.co.ke previously reported that the court ordered the singer to pay her ex-husband KSh 21.7 million for spousal and child support.
