Rebuttal: Juliette Lewis, pictured at a press day for her TV series three weeks ago is not in the nude photos

A nude photograph of a woman posted on the Internet earlier this week is not Juliette Lewis, contrary to initial reports.

In an email to website Gawker, who first posted the shot, the publicist for the 38-year-old actress wrote that: 'The image that you have on your site right now is not of her.'

The photograph features a naked woman in a compromising position with a man reported by the website to be famed photographer Terry Richardson.

Asking that the shot be 'taken down immediately,' the publicist added: 'It is correct that she did a shoot with Terry several years ago but this is absolutely not a shot from that shoot nor does this image even have her in it.'

The image of the woman, who bears a passing resemblance to Lewis quickly spread to other websites.

Sources close to the photographer had informed Gawker that the photo was 'definitely taken' at Richardson's studio a few years ago before the décor was changed. 

Lewis, whose last big movie was the 2009 roller derby film Whip It!, directed by Drew Barrymore, currently appears in the TV series version of legal thriller The Firm. 

The series takes place ten years after the book by John Grisham  and subsequent 1993 film  which starred Tom Cruise as Harvard educated lawyer Mitch McDeere. 

Grisham reportedly oversaw the first few episodes of filming to ensure he was happy with the development.
