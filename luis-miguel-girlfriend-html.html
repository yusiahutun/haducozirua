Who Is Luis Miguels Girlfriend? His Dating History Explored

 Posted on July 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  409 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Luis Miguel and his girlfriend, Mollie Hannah Gould, began dating in July 2019. At the time, the Latin artist’s relationship got a lot of attention because of the 29 year age gap between him and his girlfriend.

She is a model, dancer, and show girl. The two reportedly met when she was hired by Miguel’s team during one of his shows. And though the singer and his girlfriend have made their relationship public, they prefer to keep it out of the public eye.

Luis Miguel’s Past Girlfriends

Though Miguel has never had a wife, he has been in a relationship with several well-known personalities over the years. Back in 1998, he started dating Mariah Carey while she was in the process of divorcing her ex-husband, Tommy Motola.

The two singers met in Aspen, Colorado, when they were set up on a blind date. Though the pair did not reveal much about their relationship for a long time, Carey opened up about it in her autobiography, The Meaning of Mariah Carey.

Carey shared that she and Miguel understood each other because they shared the same profession.

The public and the media, whatever, will attack a relationship and make it into, you know, drive people crazy. And it takes someone really secure to be able to handle that kind of thing. So I’m really actually very happy in a relationship right now with… very big Latin superstar. His name is Luis Miguel.

Miguel and Carey went their separate ways after three years of dating in 2001. One reason for their split was the cultural gap between them.

Following his split from Carey, Miguel began dating Aracely Arambula in 2005. During their time together, the pair welcomed two children together — son Miguel Arambula on January 1, 2007 and son Daniel Arambula on December 18, 2008.

Though Miguel and Arambula split in 2009, she described their time together as beautiful, during an interview with Suelta La Sopa.

They were six beautiful years and the best of my life in relation to family because of the birth of my children. It was something that happened that way. I didn’t look for it. They are things of destiny and life because I have two kids.

Aside from his two sons with Arambula, he also has a daughter named Michelle Gallego with actress Stephanie Salas. Michelle was born on June 13, 1989, and Miguel was just 19 when he became a father for the first time.
