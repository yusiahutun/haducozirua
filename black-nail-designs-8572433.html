13 Black Nail Designs for the Perfect Edgy Manicure

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  319 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Black nails are the ultimate statement, whether dark and moody is your everyday aesthetic or you're looking for tips that say "Not today, Valentine's Day." If you've been considering a black manicure, winter is the time to get one—and we're not just talking about simple solids; black polish can be used to create some of the most beautiful designs. So before spring comes around and bright pastels flood your feeds, scroll through these 13 black nail ideas to try next.

Centered Sparkles

Another perfectly balanced manicure, this one features clean polish on the thumb and pinky fingers with pointed French flanking a single sparkly middle finger.

Gothic Valentine

If you're not into pinks and reds but still want to feel the love, this black and white version also does the trick.

Galactic Matte

For those astrologically inclined, try this matte look on almond nails—another no-fail shape you're likely seeing everywhere.

Floral Matte

If your season of choice wavers between winter and spring, try this matte floral design in a neutral palette of black, white, and beige.

Short & Sweet

Nothing beats a classic! These nails were shaped and shortened to just the right length for a clean, glossy polish.

Moody Prints

Nothing makes black polish pop like negative space, especially when alternating between solid lacquer and abstract prints.

Dainty Hearts

Speaking of negative space, it's also the ideal canvas for sweet little hearts in black, white, and gold.

Chain Link

We love it when goth and glam meet. The silver chain design here adds just the right amount of detail and elongates the extra-pointy nail shape.

Almond Matte

Meet the Jordan almond's dark, mysterious cousin—so smooth and pretty, you'd almost want to take a bite.

Pantyhose French

You know you see it, too. Tights are a wintertime staple, and this chic polka-dot French manicure channels the same energy.
