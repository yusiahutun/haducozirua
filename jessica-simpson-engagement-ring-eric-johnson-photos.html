Simpson Steps Out with Fiance

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  114 words  |  Martina Birk

Jessica Simpson stepped out with her two hottest accessories last night: Fiance Eric Johnson and her brand new engagement ring!

Jess was all smiles with Eric last night at LAX, where we all got a better look at the ruby rock she's been wearing on her ring finger since the football player popped the question.

Simpson talked about the proposal today on "The Early Show," saying it was "a total surprise" and "I first said 'Yes!' I didn't even try the ring on until 5 minutes in, I just sat there and cried with him."

This will be Simpson's second marriage. She was previously hitched to Nick Lachey, who also just got engaged.
