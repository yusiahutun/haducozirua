How to get M-Kopa phones: Qualifications and daily repayments

 Posted on February 7, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1519 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Do you dream of owning a smartphone but don't have enough money to buy one? M-Kopa has got you sorted. You can own your dream phone if you can afford to pay the deposit. Then, you will be required to make daily repayments for the remaining balance for one year. But do you know how to get M-Kopa phones?

M-Kopa is a connected asset financing platform that has revolutionized how Kenyans access smartphones. The company was established in 2010. M-Kopa offers an easy payment plan, making it possible for Kenyans from all walks of life to own a smartphone. But how much do you know about the repayment plans?

How to get M-Kopa phones

M-Kopa phones are available in different models and sizes at affordable prices. You can own any M-Kopa phone if you meet the minimum qualification.

If you are stuck on how to check if you qualify for an M-Kopa phone, there is no need to panic. Here is a simple procedure you should follow to own your dream M-Kopa phone:

  • You should visit your nearest Mkopa branch or agent.
  • Ensure you have your national IDand a Safaricom line when you visit the agent.
  • You will be provided with a list of phones you can purchase from M-Kopa.
  • Check each device's minimum deposit amount required and the daily instalment amount.
  • Then, choose the one that suits your budget and pay the deposit.
  • You will be allowed to walk away with the phone.
  • Once you have purchased a phone, it will be activated within 24 hours.
  • From then on, you should pay the required daily instalment for one year.

M-Kopa phones on loan

Which phones do M-Kopa offer? There are several Nokia and Samsung phones you can own under this arrangement. Each phone has got its own unique features, deposit amount, and daily payments. According to M-Kopa Kenya, these are some of the phones you can purchase on credit.

1. Nokia C21

This is one of the cheapest phones on M-Kopa. Its features include 2GB RAM, 32GB Internal Storage, a 3000 mAh Battery, an Android 11 operating system, 8MP main camera, a 6.52-inch screen, and a 5MP selfie camera.

The total cost of the phone is Kshs 25,599. If you want to own the phone, you should deposit Kshs 3,699 and make daily payments of Kshs 60 for one year.

This is one of the best M-Kopa phones you can buy. Its features include 3GB RAM, 32GB Internal Storage, a 5000 mAh Battery, an Android 10 operating system, 48MP main camera, a 6.5-inch screen, and a 5MP selfie camera.

The phone sells for Kshs 30,549 under the plan. You will be required to make a deposit of Kshs 4,999 and daily instalments of Kshs 70 for a period of one year.
