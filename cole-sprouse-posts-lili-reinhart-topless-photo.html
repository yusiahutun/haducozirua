Cole Sprouse Shares a Stunning Topless Photo of Lili Reinhart for Her Birthday

 Posted on April 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  336 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Like any other boyfriend, Cole Sprouse celebrated his girlfriend Lili Reinhart's birthday with a sweet Instagram tribute. But perhaps unlike any other boyfriend, he shared a topless portrait of his Riverdale costar to honor the occasion.

Sprouse, 26, posted a photo this afternoon of Reinhart looking into a mirror, sans a shirt. The actress turns 22 today. "Both the birthday and the gift. My little muse, happy birthday my love," he captioned the image.

The post has already amassed over 2.6 million likes at the time of writing, about an hour after he shared it.

It's likely that Cole, actor-turned-photographer, is the one who snapped the photo, since he often posts stunning images of his girlfriend on his account. Yesterday, he shared a dramatic image of Lili in the shadows.

Days before, he shared a more candid image of him nearly kissing the actress from across the table. It was equal parts blurry and sweet.

A month ago, he also shared a beautiful close-up of Reinhart, which he simply captioned, "Humming."

When Cole turned 26 on August 4, it was Lili's turn to post a heartfelt birthday tribute. She shared an image of her beau sitting on the edge of a balcony, pointing to the camera.

"It seems as if the world would still be a stranger to me, if not for you. I’m so thankful that our paths intertwined to form this beautiful adventure~ Happy birthday, my love," she wrote in the caption.

Reinhart is adamant about keeping her personal life private from the press. ("I'm not okay talking about my relationship,” she told BAZAAR.com in July. “I'm not going to tell you my love story. That's just not appropriate right now.”) But on Instagram, her romance with Sprouse is out in the open.
