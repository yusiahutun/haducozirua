The Bollywood star hit a big year

    Priyanka Chopra marked a big milestone on Monday 18 July, turning 40 with her friends and family in attendance for a spectacular birthday bash.

    Alongside a sweet shot of them grabbing a kiss, he shared another one of hers at her party and one holding a towel that said: "Priyanka! The Jewel of July est. 1982."
