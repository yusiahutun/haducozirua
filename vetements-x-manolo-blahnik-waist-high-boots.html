Vetements x Manolo Blahnik waist-high boots Would you wear them?

 Posted on April 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  281 words  |  Martina Birk

Manolo Blahnik x Vetements - Rex

When Manolo Blahnik joined forces with the French fashion house of the moment, Vetements, the collection was going to be anything but boring.

For the brand's autumn/winter 2016 couture show last July, pairs of satin waist-high boots came down the catwalk in a rainbow of colours. Now that they have finally hit retailers, people on Twitter are somewhat losing their minds.

How would you get them over a pair of jeans? How on Earth would you go to the toilet? Are these boots or crotch-less trousers? Valid questions we're sure you'll agree about this £3,190 accessory.

Related Story

While they certainly are out there – would you expect anything less? – they already have one very predictable celebrity fan in the form of Rihanna. The singer teamed hers with cut-off denim shorts, a black crop top and a floor-length orange coat.

And if you're thinking that Kim Kardashian would probably feel right at home in them too, then you would be correct. The reality-television star was spotted wearing the black ones on Valentine's Day.

Meanwhile, on the catwalk, they were styled with lightweight, floaty dresses and casual tracksuits, tucked into one side or with one boot folded down, which does make them feel a lot more wearable than imagining them with hot pants or jeans.

Imaxtree

While they might not be the easiest shoes to pull off, they have every chance of becoming the 'It' piece of the season, so we might just have to get onboard. And if you're feeling bold enough, we suggest you snap up a pair now, before they sell out.

Satin boots, £3,190, Vetements x Manolo Blahnik at Net-A-Porter
