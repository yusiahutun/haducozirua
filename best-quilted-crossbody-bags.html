Best Weather-Friendly Bag

Lemaire Small Soft Game Bag

Best Weather-Friendly Bag

Lemaire Small Soft Game Bag

There are a few things I love about this unusual quilted bag from Lemaire. Firstly, it's made of nylon, which means that it's water-repellant (and as such, winter weather friendly!). And second, the soft U-shape and adjustable strap allow it to be worn tucked under the shoulder or across the body.

Size: 16"W x 10"H x 4.5"D

Material: Nylon

Color: Green
