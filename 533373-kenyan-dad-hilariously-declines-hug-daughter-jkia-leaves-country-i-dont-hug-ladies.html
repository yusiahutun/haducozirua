Kenyan Dad Hilariously Declines to Embrace Daughter at JKIA as She Flies Out: &quot;I Don't Hug Ladies&quot;

 Posted on February 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  442 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Peris Muthoni, a Kenyan nurse in the UK, shared a TikTok video of her dad's hilarious reaction at JKIA, and it has since elicited funny reactions
  • Her family gathered to bid her farewell when she asked her dad for a hug, but he comically refused, claiming he doesn't hug ladies
  • The video sparked a discussion in the comments, with Kenyans sharing their own experiences trying to get hugs from their dads

A Kenyan lady has left netizens in laughter after sharing a heartwarming video showing her dad declining to hug her at the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

The woman, Peris Muthoni, a Kenyan nurse working in the United Kingdom, was at the JKIA bidding farewell to her family, who had seen her off when her dad amused them.

In the video shared on TikTok, Muthoni asked her dad to hug her, but he funnily declined, saying he does not hug ladies.
