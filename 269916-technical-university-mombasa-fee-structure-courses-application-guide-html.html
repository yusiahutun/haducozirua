The Technical University of Mombasa application guide

Application forms can be obtained from the Office of the Registrar (AA) at the Technical University of Mombasa (TUM) or downloaded from the TUM Website. Below is a simple Technical University of Mombasa application guide:

  • Click "APPLICATION Form-revised 1-8-2023" to download the form to your device.
  • Complete the application form and print it out.
  • Submit the completed TUM application form with all supporting documents to the Academic Affairs Registrar before the application deadline.
  • Attach the original application fee and 50% of the 1st-semester fees bank slips when submitting the application form and during registration.
  • The COVID-19 protocol scanned documents to be sent to admissions@tum.ac.ke.
  • Your duly completed TUM application forms, supporting documents, and bank slips should be addressed and undersigned to:

Registrar Academic Affairs,

P.O. BOX 90420 - 80100 G.P.O. MOMBASA

What is TUM's application fee?

PhD applicants pay Kshs. 2,000, while master's applicants pay Kshs. 1,500. Bachelor's applicants pay Kshs. 1,000, while diplomas & certificate applicants pay Kshs. 500 as the application fee. Additionally, all applicants are required to pay a non-refundable fee of Kshs. 3,000. All successful MID-ENTRY applicants shall be required to pay a mid-entry (credit transfer) fee of KShs. 15,000 at registration.

What are TUM's application deadlines?

Duly completed application forms, supporting documents, and fee bank slips should be submitted by 31st December 2023 for the January 2024 intake, 30th April 2024 for the May 2024 intake, and 30th August 2024 for the September 2024 intake.

Degree/diploma KUCCPS-placed students can download admission letters on TUM's website. Follow this guide:

  • Visit TUM's website.
  • Click "Portals."
  • Select "Student's Portals."
  • Select "E-Registration Portal."
  • Enter your KCSE Index number as your username.
  • Enter your password and click "Sign In."
  • The Admission Letter Download Screen will prompt you to download your letter.
  • Click "Download now."
  • In case of missing details, the screen will prompt you to provide your details.
  • Fill out your details, contact details, and next of kin details.
  • Scan and attach the required documents in PDF format.
  • Enter or select how you came to know about TUM.
  • Click "Submit" to send your application.
  • Check your admission letter in the download folder named AdmissionLetter_Out.pdf.

Does the Technical University of Mombasa offer accommodation?

Hostels at the Technical University of Mombasa cost Kshs. 6,750 per semester. Submit a hostel application form to TUM's accommodation manager's office. After that, book a room via TUM's student portal.

Is TUM private or public?

The Technical University of Mombasa (TUM) is a public university and one of Kenya's oldest higher learning institutions.

What is TUM's address?

The Technical University of Mombasa's location is along Tom Mboya Street in Mombasa, Kenya.

How many branches does the Technical University of Mombasa have?

TUM's main campus is in Mombasa along Tom Mboya Street. The university has two satellite campuses, one in Kwale and another in Lamu counties.

Is the Technical University of Mombasa accredited?

TUM is recognized by the Commission for University Education (CUE) in Kenya as a coeducational Kenyan higher education institution.

How do you check if a university is accredited in Kenya?

Is the Technical University of Mombasa under TVET?

What are the Technical University of Mombasa's cluster points?
