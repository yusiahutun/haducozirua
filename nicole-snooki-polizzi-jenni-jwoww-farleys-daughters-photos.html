Nicole Snooki Polizzi, Jenni JWoww Farleys Daughters' Photos

 Posted on June 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  129 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi and Jenni “JWoww” Farley’s daughters are friends just like their moms.

JWoww welcomed daughter Meilani in July 2014 with then-husband Roger Mathews. The former couple — who also share son Grayson, born in 2016 — called it quits in 2019. Snooki, for her part, shares three children with husband Jionni LaValle: Lorenzo, born in 2012, Giovanna, born in 2014, and Angelo, born in 2019.

In April 2021, JWoww exclusively told Us Weekly that her and Snooki’s daughters “love the cameras” just like their mothers, who are both OG cast members of Jersey Shore. She added that Meilani and Giovanna “FaceTime each other almost every day.”
