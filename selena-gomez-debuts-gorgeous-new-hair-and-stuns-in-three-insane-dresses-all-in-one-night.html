Selena Gomez debuts 'gorgeous' new hair and stuns in three insane dresses - all in one night

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  454 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Calm Down, Selena Gomez has just debuted her stunning hair transformation, as she showcases her sleek bob. Not only that, but she wore three iconic outfits in one night. We can’t cope.

The music star hosted the inaugural Rare Impact Fund Benefit in Hollywood this week, raising money to expand access to mental health services and education for young people. Let’s take a look at all her stunning looks.

Selena Gomez stuns in three different looks

Kicking off the night, the former Disney Channel star wowed in a sparkly silver gown with a long train. To add to the wow factor, the custom Valentino included a rose detail at the top.

Sticking with the flower theme, Selena Gomez then changed into a custom Rahul Mishra “iris” couture dress.

In his own words on Instagram, Mishra said: “The look aims to imitate the petals of an Iris flower through intricate hand embroidery.”

Last, but certainly not least was a simple, yet oh-so-effective, black minidress by Monse. After the red carpet looks, it was time for the singer to make her way inside to thank guests for their support.

Taking to the stage, the Single Soon singer’s long sleeve dress stayed with the floral theme with patterns of nature and animals.

Selena’s dresses weren’t the only thing she changed

After the three flawless looks, Selena’s hair may not have been the first thing fans noticed. However, she also debuted her ‘hair transformation’ as she showed off a sleek bob, and we’re here for it.

Fans over on X, formerly known as Twitter were also here for the look as one penned: “OMG Selena’s hair is GORGEOUS!’

Her sleek locks sit just above her shoulders, making a change from the long black locks she’s been walking red carpets with recently, including the MTV VMAs.

Although she’s known for her black hair, the star has stepped outside the box before. In 2017 the 31-year-old debuted blonde hair on the AMA’s red carpet.

Once again, in 2021, she went platinum blonde which she revealed via her beauty brand, Rare Beauty. Since then, it seems she’s stuck to black, although in 2023 she did post a photo dump where her hair was seemingly blonde.

Although, we all know the work that goes into that so we can safely assume that was just a wig.

After addressing their ‘beef’ early this year, Selena’s celebrity friend, and kidney donor Francia Raisa also attended the event, matching in a silver gown.

However, it looks like it wasn’t actually planned, as the How I Met Your Mother star told Extra: “The only thing we coordinated was our spray tans, [which we got together]. We didn’t talk about our dress.”
