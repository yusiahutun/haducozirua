Appointments | South China Morning Post

 Posted on July 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  296 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ludwig Reichhold has been appointed general manager of Dragages.

He will be responsible for the company's activities in Hong Kong.

Mr Reichhold joined the Bouygues Group in 1979 and, since 1992, has been in charge of the Dragages' building works in Hong Kong and E & M subsidiary BYME engineering.Johnson Stokes & Master (in association with Norton Rose) has appointed Simon S O Ip as senior partner.

He will take over from Peter Edwards, who will retire from the partnership but will remain with the firm as a consultant.

Mr Ip has been with JSM since 1972 and has been a partner in the litigation department since 1977. As head of the litigation department, he specialised in civil and commercial litigation and disputes.New York Life Insurance Worldwide has appointed Chris Au as director, agency development, and Paul Siu as manager of the new business department.

Mr Au will be responsible for agency recruitment and development of managers.

Mr Siu will be responsible for ensuring underwriting standards and practices.

TNT Express Worldwide HK has named Eric Wong as general sales manager.

Mr Wong will be responsible for sales, specifically focusing on expansion of intra-Asia and worldwide air express business, as well as Mailfast, an international mailing service.

Financial Times Information, a supplier of financial data, business news and analysis, has appointed Jonathan Schmidt as director of general business information, Asia Pacific, and managing director, China.

Since 1994, Mr Schmidt has served as managing director of Chamber World Network, an on-line information provider owned by the Association of German Chambers of Commerce and Industry.

Gartner Group/Dataquest Asia Pacific has named Dr Kishore Pasumarty as senior industry consultant with Gartner Group's Worldwide Consulting.

Based in Singapore, Dr Pasumarty will develop and conduct consulting projects in the Asia Pacific.
