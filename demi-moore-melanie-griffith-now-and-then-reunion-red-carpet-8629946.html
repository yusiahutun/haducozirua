See Demi Moore and Melanie Griffith's 'Now and Then' Reunion

 Posted on February 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  299 words  |  Martina Birk

Demi Moore and Melanie Griffith are still all for one, and one for all. 

Nearly 30 years after the '90s girlhood film Now and Then first came out, co-stars Moore and Griffith had a mini reunion at the "Unforgettable Evening" event benefitting the Women's Cancer Research Fund in Beverly Hills on Wednesday. Catching up on the red carpet, Moore, who played Samantha Albertson in the film, and Griffith, who took on the role of Tina "Teeny" Tercell, proved they're still the best of friends. In photos, the two sweetly held hands as they posed for the camera, and, at one point, they cracked up laughing as if no time had passed. 

For the event, both ladies looked stylish, with Griffith wearing a white pantsuit with a ruffled button-down underneath her tailored jacket. She accessorized with lacy fingerless gloves, pearl stud earrings, and a pair of nude platform sandals, while her glam consisted of a choppy blonde bob and pink lipgloss. 

Meanwhile, Moore opted for a black halter-neck gown that featured a sexy slit down the side, revealing a pair of strappy sandals below. She finished off her look with diamond chain-link earrings, a blingy bracelet, and her long dark hair styled in loose waist-length waves. 

Coincidentally, their co-star Rita Wilson (Chrissy) also attended the same event with her husband, Tom Hands, and took a quick photo with Moore along with the Women's Cancer Research Fund co-founder Jamie Tisch.   

Moore, Griffith, and Wilson all starred in Now and Then alongside Rosie O'Donnell (Roberta), as well as Christina Ricci, Thora Birch, Ashleigh Aston Moore, and Gaby Hoffmann, who play teen versions of the four women. The movie looks back on an unforgettable summer shared between the group of friends in 1970 and their reunion two decades later.
