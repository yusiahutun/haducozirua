Brad Paisley Launching Moonshine Spirit Clothing Line

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  266 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Brad Paisley can officially add Fashion Designer to his resume. The 'Perfect Storm' singer has paired up with Boot Barn to create the Moonshine Spirit Line, which includes boots, jeans, hats and more.

Boot Barn is a prominent western and work wear outfitter, and they'll only continue to grow now that they've enlisted Paisley to help with design. The appropriately-named line will include a wide array of clothing and accessories including boots, jeans, hats, T-shirts, jewelry, belts and woven shirts. The goal is to use the signature Boot Barn style while adding a "modern, country music edge."

"We specifically chose Brad Paisley to create this line because his values align beautifully with those of our customers and employees," Boot Barnn's Chief Executive Officer Jim Conroy says in a release. "He is authentic, and that's the essence of every Boot Barn customer."

The line will target men ages 18-40 who are looking for something contemporary. It won't all be workwear, however. The country star has drawn up designs for dressier items which include boots and hats. Prices start at $20 for some of the merchandise, but expect to pay $299 or more for a leather jacket.

"Boot Barn and I have created what I think is an authentic and very wearable line of country clothing," Paisley explains. "It's hip, modern, progressive, rugged and country -- ready for the stage and ready to go out on the town. It's a great time to launch Moonshine Spirit by Brad Paisley. It embodies the enthusiasm of my new album, 'Moonshine in the Trunk.'"
