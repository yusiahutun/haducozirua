Taylor Swift’s influence on the NFL is undeniable. The official NFL Instagram shared a cartoon of some of the top athletes in partnership with Nickelodeon. It also includes a cutest depiction of Travis Kelce – and it has a Taylor Swift connection.

NFL hiding Taylor Swift Easter egg with Travis Kelce is actually adorable
