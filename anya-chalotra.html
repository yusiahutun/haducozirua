Anya Chalotra is an Indo-English actress who earned the majority of her fans after she was seen starring in “The Witcher” a Netflix original series. She has also played a few theatre roles and other Netflix series. One of her remarkable performances was in the short TV film based on the testimonies of the key frontline workers in East London in this situation of the global pandemic. She has also played a voice role in the animated series “NEX-GEN” and will be seen podcasting on BBC Sounds’ upcoming show ‘The Cipher’.

Early Life

Belongs to Hindu descent, Anya was born on 21st July 1996 in Lower Penn, South Staffordshire. She spent a harmonious childhood while growing up with her Indo-English parents, her father Madan Chalotra being Indian, and her mother April Chalotra English along with her siblings Reeya and Arun.

Anya grew up following the Hindu culture and completed her elementary studies at Brewood in St. Dominic’s Grammer School. After that, she attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art for a one-year foundation course and also attended the Guildhall School of Music and Drama for enhancing her skills.

Anya Chalotra Career

Anya has enhanced her skills from the very beginning of her life as she attended dramatic schools and training centers to start her career properly. She played in some theatre productions by West End Theatre namely ‘Much Ado About Nothing’ and ‘The Villages.
