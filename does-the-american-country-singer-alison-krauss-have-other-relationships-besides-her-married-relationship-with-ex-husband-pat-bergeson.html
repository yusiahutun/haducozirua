Alison Krauss: Quick Facts

Alison Krauss 

  • Alison Maria Krauss was born on July 23, 1971, in Decatur, Illinois, United States.
  • She is the daughter of a German immigrant father Fred Krauss and Italian descent mother Louise Krauss.
  • At an early age of 10, she won a local singing contest and recorded her first song at 14 years old.
  • Alison signed with Rounder Records in 1985 and released her first solo album in 1987.
  • As of 2018, she bagged 27 Grammy Awards from 42 other nominations.
  • Krauss was once diagnosed with vocal condition dysphonia. 
  • Krauss grew up listening to everything from folk to opera to pop and rock music.
