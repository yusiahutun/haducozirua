Taylor Swift nervously signed fan's arm but people pick up on how she's holding the pen

 Posted on March 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Nothing can come between Taylor Swift and her fans, including when it comes to nervously signing an autograph – but Swifties had thoughts on how she was holding her pen in the unearthed clip.

The video in question, from the premiere of the concert movie for Eras Tour, saw the singer have a special moment with one fan who had been waiting in line. The celebrity looked stunning in her blue gown and tousled waves, which she pinned underneath for a fake bob look. The movie has been record-breaking, just like the world tour itself. As of February, it took in $261m worldwide, making it the best performing concert film of all time. The Guinness World Records also recognized the amazing milestone as well. It goes hand in hand with Taylor Swift‘s Eras Tour that’s already racked up at least $1 billion.

Particular request from fan

In the video, we see the music star walk towards a Swiftie holding out a pen, she has a handful of friendship bracelets, (of course), as she asks her to confirm what she wants to do.

“You want what?”

The fan says: “Can you sign my arm for a tattoo?”

“Oh, so it’s very special,” she replies, as the fan tells the celebrity her entire arm is all Taylor Swift related ink.

Then the listener says: “You’re beautiful, Taylor,” but the 34-year-old is more concerned about how precise the lines are coming out with the permanent marker.

“Just hold really still,” she insists, smiling. “There’s a lot of pressure on the tattoo”.

But the Swiftie believed in her regardless, saying she’s ‘got it’, knowing she could do it, even if the star – who is dating Travis Kelce – seemed nervous.

Taylor Swift fans loved how she was holding the pen

The cute moment was too much to ignore, and fans couldn’t help but notice how Taylor Swift was holding the pen, in between her pointer and middle finger.

One fan wrote: “I’m sorry but I LOVE the way she holds a writing utensil. I have officially trained myself to start writing just like that lol”.

A second echoed: “The way she holds the Sharpie is so cute”.

And another wrote: “Taylor holds her pen the same way that I do! I wonder if she had a cast in first/second grade too”.

Others commented on how sweet the moment was between the pair.

Someone believed: “Legit, that’s so chill of her, because imagine how much she could’ve charged $$$ for that personalized tattoo”.

“She’s a true angel,” declared another.
