Who is Busta Rhymes?

Busta is an American professional rapper and actor. He was born on May 20th, 1972, as Trevor John Smith Jr., to Geraldine Green and Trevor Smith Sr. Both parents are from Jamaica. His friend, Chuck D of Public Enemy, gave him the moniker Busta Rhymes, after NFL and CFL wide receiver George "Buster" Rhymes.
