Discover the best free child places 2023 TUI has to offer for your summer holiday

    TUI’s free child places are back for 2023, and there’s thousands of holiday destinations to choose from where you won’t pay a penny for your little one. If ever there was incentive to book your next trip for the school summer holidays, it’s that!

    BOOK NOW

    As we’re all trying to save a bit of cash this year, TUI’s latest offer means families can still enjoy a summer holiday but without the huge price tag. 

    There’s holidays available to book from now until September, so that family summer holiday you've been dreaming of doesn’t have to be off the cards with TUI’s big savings.

    What’s included in TUI’s free kids places? 

    One child per two full-paying adults can stay for free in selected hotels with the TUI free child place holiday offer. They’ll get a standard flight ticket, transfers to and from your accommodation included plus whichever board basis is offered at your hotel of choice. 

    The holiday deals can be booked with low deposits and monthly payment plans, with departures from 21 airports including Manchester, Belfast, Bristol and London Stansted. 

    One free kid’s place is available in each selected hotel room or selected apartment type or villa, with a minimum of two full paying adults.

    Is the TUI offer available in the school summer holidays?

     The TUI free child places are available to book for holidays now and those in the school holidays too, including the May half term and the big school summer holiday during July and August too. 

    What are the best TUI family friendly resorts in the offer? 

    TUI is a treasure trove of brilliantly family friendly hotels with five-star reviews, from Spanish all-inclusive mega resorts to boutique Greek hotels, not to mention Turkey’s incredible array of resorts geared towards families too.
