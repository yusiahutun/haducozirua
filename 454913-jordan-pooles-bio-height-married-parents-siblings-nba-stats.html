Jordan Poole is an American basketball player who plays for the Golden State Warriors in the National Basketball Association. He has been playing as a shooting and point guard for the Warriors since he joined the team in 2019.

During the 2021-22 NBA season, he led the entire league in free throw percentage at 92.5%, making him one of the most improved players in the NBA. Poole has been in the NBA for three seasons and has reached the playoffs once. However, he is yet to win a title or make it to the All-Star.

Early life

The player was born Jordan Anthony Poole on 19 June 1999 (age 22 as of 2022) in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, United States. He is the son of Monet Poole (mother) and Anthony Poole (father) and has a brother and sister.

Jordan Poole attended Rufus King High School in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, where he started playing basketball. He played for the institution’s basketball team and visited several states as a blue-chip high school basketball recruit.

Poole did not complete his studies at Rufus King High School. Instead, he announced he would transfer to play his senior basketball season for La Lumiere School in La Porte, Indiana. At Lumiere School, he was part of the 2016 first team All-Wisconsin selection league as a junior. He helped the school reach the 2017 Dick's National High School Championships.

In 2017, Jordan joined the University of Michigan and played for the institution for two years before getting drafted in 2019. Between 2017 and 2019, he played 75 games for the Michigan Wolverines men's basketball team.

One of his most favourable moments at the university was when he hit a buzzer-beating three-pointer in the second round of the 2018 NCAA Tournament. The win gave his team the fourth Sweet 16 in six years. After two years of playing for Michigan, Jordan averaged 9.4 points per game and 2.2 rebounds.

Professional career

After playing for the University of Michigan between 2017 and 2019, Jordan joined the Golden State Warriors after the team drafted him 28th overall in the first round of the 2019 NBA Draft.

He then signed a guaranteed contract of $6.2 million over three years. On 11 July 2019, the Warriors signed him to his rookie scale contract. He has played against the following teams:

Los Angeles Clippers

Poole debuted in the NBA on 24 October 2019 during the 2019-20 season. He came off the bench to play a game where his team lost 122-141 to the Los Angeles Clippers. However, he put some good stats, including five points, two rebounds, two assists, and a steal.

New Orleans

On 29 October 2019, Jordan started his first game for the Warriors against the New Orleans Pelicans. He scored 13 points and helped his team get its first win of the season. In December 2019, the Warriors assigned him to the Santa Cruz Warriors of the NBA G-League.
