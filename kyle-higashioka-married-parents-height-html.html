Kyle Higashioka is an American professional baseball catcher who has been part of the New York Yankees since 2008 representing them in the Major League Baseball (MLB). He was on the 40-man roster after hitting .276 with 21 homers and 81 RBIs in 2016/17 season.

The baseball player who was drafted in the seventh round of the 2008 MLB draft made his professional MLB debut from Yankees in 2017.

Wiki (Age), Bio

Kyle was born on 20 April 1990 in Huntington Beach, California, USA. He stands at the height of 1.85 m (6 feet 1 inch) with a muscular body build and has a weight that touches approximately 92 kg margin.

The 29-years-old MLB star attended Edison High School in Huntington and played for the school's baseball team. For further education, he enrolled in the University of California, Berkeley where he played college baseball for the California Golden Bears. 

Kyle thought about learning Japanese which would help him to communicate with his teammate Masahiro Tanaka, the ace of the Yankees'  pitching staff. He has also learned Spanish in his high school years, which has helped him to communicate with his Latin American teammates.

Family Background

Talking about Kyle's family background, the Yankees' star is a fourth-generation Japanese-American from his father’s side.

His father, Ted Higashioka, is a third-generation Japanese American who spent a few years in Japan. After returning to the US, his father didn’t have much reason for communication in Japanese. Ted can understand the language but does not feel comfortable speaking it.

Apart from that, Kyle is pretty much close to both of his parents. He has promised his mother that he would earn a college degree and is currently studying Mechanical Engineering degree at Orange Coast College.

The MLB star is the eldest son of the family and has a younger brother.

Kyle’s grandfather, Shigeru Higashioka, worked as an oil company executive in Japan. Shigeru was awarded a Bronze Star in the Army's 442nd Infantry Regiment; the unit was covered with mostly Japanese-American group that fought in Europe during World War II.
