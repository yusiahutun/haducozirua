Biography

Barrie Chase was born in New York on October 20, 1933. Film and television actress and dancer is known for her appearances in such Hollywood movies as Brigadoon, Pal Joey, Daddy Long Legs, and It are a Mad, Mad, Mad, Mad World. Her television credits include dance performances on The Colgate Comedy Hour and Bonanza. She appeared with Hollywood star James Stewart in the 1965 20th Century Fox movie The Flight of the Phoenix. She began her career by performing with the renowned dancer Fred Astaire in several 1950s and 1960s television specials.
